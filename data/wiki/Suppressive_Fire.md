In Suppressive Fire, the soldier is more intent on saturating the combat area with gunfire, rather than actually inflicting damage to the enemy forces.

The goal of Suppressive Fire is to restrict the capacity of the enemy forces to maneuver or successfully return fire, by unleashing a barrage of overwhelming gunfire upon them.

SUPPRESSIVE FIRE

LONG SKILL

[Attack](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user must select a weapon with the **[Suppressive Fire Trait](/Suppressive_Fire_Trait "Suppressive Fire Trait")**.

EFFECTS

*   The user enters the [Suppressive Fire State](/Suppressive_Fire_State "Suppressive Fire State"), using the selected weapon.
*   [G: Jumper](/G:_Jumper "G: Jumper") and Suppressive Fire:
    *   If a Trooper with G: Jumper has a Proxy in Suppressive Fire State, that Proxy stays in Suppressive Fire State even if another Proxy is activated.
