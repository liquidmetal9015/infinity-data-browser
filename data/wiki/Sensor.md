Hyper-developed senses and sophisticated tracking technology are the two sides of the Sensor coin. Either way, the bottom line is the same: the ability to uncover hidden threats in one's immediate surroundings.

SENSOR

SHORT SKILL

[Attack, Optional, Zone of Control.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   By declaring Sensor, the user may make a [Normal](/Normal "Normal") [WIP](/WIP "WIP")+6 Roll (without applying [Range](/Range "Range") or [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism") MODs) to simultaneously [Discover](/Discover "Discover") all enemy Troopers, Weapons or pieces of Equipment in the [Hidden Deployment](/Hidden_Deployment_State "Hidden Deployment State") or [Camouflaged](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged") States, who are inside the user's Zone of Control.
*   The user does not need [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to a target, or to designate a target at all.
*   Enemies with the [Camouflage](/Camouflage "Camouflage") Special Skill cannot regain Camouflaged State inside the [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") of the user.
*   Sensor also grants its user, automatically and without making Rolls or spending Orders, a +6 MOD to WIP when declaring Discover against [Camouflage Markers](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged").
