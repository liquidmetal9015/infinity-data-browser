The Armed Turret is a Deployable Weapon designed to be deployed on the battlefield to react to the enemy.

ARMED TURRET

[Disposable (1), Deployable, Non-Reloadable, Perimeter.](/Traits "Traits")

EFFECTS

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

*   These weapons are placed on the game table using the [Place Deployable](/Place_Deployable "Place Deployable") Common Skill, applying the [Perimeter and Deployable](/Place_Deployable#Deployable_and_Perimeter "Place Deployable") rule.  
    Therefore, when players deploy this weapon, they place it totally inside the [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") of the Trooper, instead of placing it in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact").

*   The Armed Turret is a [Deployable Weapon](/Deployable_Weapon "Deployable Weapon"), declaring a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") or [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") against any Order declared by an active Enemy Model (but not a Marker) within its [LoF](/LoF "LoF") or in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact").
*   The Armed Turret's BS Weapon is listed in brackets after the weapon's name in the Trooper's Unit Profile.
*   The Armed Turret must be removed from the gaming table when it suffers a number of [Wounds](/Wounds "Wounds") equal or higher than its [STR](/STR "STR") Attribute value.
*   **Restriction:** The Armed Turret is stationary and cannot move.
*   Due to the Deployable Trait, this weapon does not activate other Deployable Weapons or Equipment.
