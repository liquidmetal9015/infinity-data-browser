Under certain circumstances, Troopers can declare this Common Skill to move about the battlefield without attracting enemy AROs.

CAUTIOUS MOVEMENT

LONG SKILL

[Movement, No Roll.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   Allows the Trooper to move up to the first value of his [MOV](/MOV "MOV") [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute"), generating no enemy [AROs](/ARO "ARO") in the process.
*   **Restriction.** For this Cautious Movement to generate no [AROs](/ARO "ARO"), it must begin and end at two points on the game table that lie outside the [LoF](/LoF "LoF") and [ZoC](/ZoC "ZoC") of all enemy Models and Markers.
*   If the Cautious Movement begins or ends within the LoF or ZoC of any enemy Trooper (in Model form) or Marker, the Trooper generates AROs as usual.
*   **Restriction.** For the Cautious Movement of a **[Hackable](/Hackable "Hackable") Trooper in [Model](/Model "Model") form** to generate no AROs, it must also begin and end at two points on the game table that lie outside the [Hacking Area](/Hacking_Area "Hacking Area") of any [enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") [Hackers](/Hacker "Hacker").
*   If a **Hackable Trooper is in Model form** and its Cautious Movement begins or ends inside the Hacking Area of any enemy Hackers, the Trooper generates AROs as usual.  
    If a **non**\-Hackable Trooper is in Model form and its Cautious Movement begins or ends inside the Hacking Area of any enemy Hackers, this does **not** cause the Trooper to generate AROs.
*   Cautious Movement must follow the [General Movement Rules](/General_Movement_Rules "General Movement Rules") as well as the [Moving and Measuring](/Moving_and_Measuring "Moving and Measuring") rules.

  

IMPORTANT

This Common Skill cannot be declared by [TAGs, Remotes, Vehicles](/Unit_Profile#Trooper_Characteristics "Unit Profile"), Troopers with [Motorcycles](/Motorcycle "Motorcycle"), Troopers with the [Aerial](/Aerial "Aerial") Special Skill, or Troopers in [Targeted State](/Targeted_State "Targeted State").

  

REMEMBER

Troopers in [Hidden Deployment](/Hidden_Deployment "Hidden Deployment") are neither Models nor Markers, so they cannot react to a Cautious Movement by having [LoF](/LoF "LoF") or being in [ZoC](/ZoC "ZoC").

  

SEE ALSO

**[Stealth](/Stealth "Stealth")**

*   If the user declares Cautious Movement, [Idle](/Idle "Idle"), or a Basic Short Skill with the [Movement Label](/Movement_Label "Movement Label"), they only grant [AROs](/ARO "ARO") to enemies that have [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to them.

  

Cautious Movement Example

A PanOceanian Trooper in Model form intends to close in on a Nomad Trooper. Since the Trooper is in [Total Cover](/Total_Cover "Total Cover") behind a tall building, outside the [LoF](/LoF "LoF") and [ZoC](/ZoC "ZoC") of the Nomad Troopers and an enemy [Camouflage Marker](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged"), the PanOceanian Trooper uses part of their first [MOV](/MOV "MOV") value to move behind a container where they will also be outside the LoF and ZoC of the Nomad Troopers and the Camouflage Marker.

No Nomad Troopers, including the Camouflage Marker, can declare [AROs](/ARO "ARO"), since the PanOceanian Trooper's Movement begins and ends outside their LoF and ZoC.
