This Special Skill represents the capacity of certain Troopers to heal from damage they have received, by removing Wounds.

![regenerate0.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/regenerate0.jpg)

REGENERATION

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[States Phase, Optional.](/Labels "Labels")

ACTIVATION

*   Automatic during the [States Phase](/States_Phase "States Phase"), if the player decides to use this Special Skill.

EFFECTS

*   During the States Phase, the user makes a [Normal](/Normal "Normal") [PH](/PH "PH") Roll:
    *   If the Roll is a success, the Trooper removes 1 [Wound](/Wound "Wound"), cancelling the [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State") if applicable.
    *   If the Roll fails, the Trooper receives 1 additional [Wound](/Wound "Wound").
*   This Special Skill remains active even when its owner is in a [Null](/Null "Null") State.

  

NOTE

If the Trooper receives a [Wound](/Wound "Wound"), the player places a Regeneration Token beside the Model, as a reminder to perform the check in the next States Phase.
