BAGGAGE

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Obligatory, No Roll.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   An [Allied](/Allied "Allied") Trooper in a Non-[Null](/Null "Null") state and inside the [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") of this Trooper may perform the [Reload](/Reload "Reload") Common Skill to either cancel their [Unloaded State](/Unloaded_State "Unloaded State"), or to regain all expended uses of their [Disposable](/Disposable "Disposable") weapons or pieces of Equipment. This effect may only be applied to a single Allied Trooper per Order.
*   In the [States Phase](/States_Phase "States Phase"), each Allied Trooper inside the Zone of Control of this Trooper may automatically cancel their Unloaded State, or regain all expended uses of their Disposable weapons or pieces of Equipment.
*   Recovering the use of Disposable weapons and pieces of Equipment does not affect any weapons and pieces of Equipment that were already deployed on the gaming table.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Non-Reloadable](/Non-Reloadable "Non-Reloadable")**  
[Unloaded State](/Unloaded_State "Unloaded State") cannot be cancelled for [Disposable](/Disposable "Disposable") weapons or pieces of Equipment with the [Non-Reloadable](/Non-Reloadable "Non-Reloadable") Label or Trait, and their expended uses cannot be regained.
