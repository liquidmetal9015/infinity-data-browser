CARBONITE

SHORT SKILL / ARO

[Comms Attack, Non-Lethal.](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The target may only be an [enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") Trooper with the [Hackable](/Hackable "Hackable") Characteristic ([HI](/HI "HI"), [REM](/REM "REM"), [TAG](/TAG "TAG"), [VH](/VH "VH")...) or an enemy [Hacker](/Hacker "Hacker").

EFFECTS

*   In the Active Turn, this Program's [Burst](/Burst "Burst") of 2 allows the user to perform two [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Rolls, which can be distributed among one or more targets as usual.
*   Each successful Roll, due to [DA Ammunition](/DA_Ammunition "DA Ammunition"), forces the target to make two [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") against [BTS](/BTS "BTS"), with PS 7.
*   Any failed Saving Roll causes the target to enter **[Immobilized-B State](/Immobilized-B_State "Immobilized-B State")**, placing an Immobilized-B State Token (IMM-B) by them.
*   A [Critical](/Critical "Critical") with Carbonite forces its target to perform an additional Saving Roll.
*   The range of this Program is the Hacker's [Hacking Area](/Hacking_Area "Hacking Area").
