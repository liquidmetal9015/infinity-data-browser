In Infinity, every Weapon has its own game profile and some of them even have special usage rules. This chapter describes how to read such a profile, and each Weapon is detailed individually in the [Weapons Chart](/Weapons_Chart "Weapons Chart"). The MULTI Rifle will be used as an example to explain how to read a BS Weapon profile.

[Suppressive Fire](/Suppressive_Fire "Suppressive Fire")

MULTI Weapons are an example of Weapons that have more than one firing mode. Each firing mode has a **[Burst](/Burst "Burst") (B)** and different **[Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition")** associated with them. The player must decide which firing mode to use each time they declare a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack").

The MULTI Rifle in AP Mode can use [AP Ammunition](/AP_Ammunition "AP Ammunition") and B3. This means that if the player declares BS Attack, they will roll 3 dice with AP Ammunition. Conversely, if they decide to use Anti-materiel Mode they will have just 1 die available, but will use [DA Ammunition](/DA_Ammunition "DA Ammunition").

  

Profile Key
-----------

**[Range](/Range "Range")** indicates the Weapon's reach in inches. In this case the MULTI Rifle has a reach of 0 to 48 inches, this reach is divided in different ranges that imply different MODs to the BS Attack Roll, as explained in the [Range](/Range "Range") Modifier section.

**[Possibility of Survival (PS)](/Possibility_of_Survival_\(PS\) "Possibility of Survival (PS)")** indicates the destructive capability of the Weapon. The lower the PS value, the more powerful the Weapon.

**[Burst](/Burst "Burst")** (B) is the number of dice the Active Player must roll when declaring a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack"). **Remember that in the Reactive Turn the value of B is 1**, unless modified by a rule or Skill.

The **[Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition")** column indicates what Type of Ammunition the Weapon uses. If two Types of Ammunition are shown linked by a "+" it means both Types are combined.

The **[Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") Attribute** column indicates which Attribute or Attributes ([ARM](/ARM "ARM"), [BTS](/BTS "BTS"), [PH](/PH "PH"), ARM+BTS, etc.) any Saving Rolls are made against to avoid the hit's damage or Effects. As with Ammunition, saving against different Attributes or their combination may be necessary (see [Possibility of Survival (PS)](/Possibility_of_Survival_\(PS\) "Possibility of Survival (PS)")). The Attribute can also be shown with MODs, usually negative (-3, -6), or it can directly show the value for that Attribute (ARM=0).

The **Number of Saving Rolls** (SR: No) column indicates the number of [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") that need to be made for every hit received.

**[Traits](/Traits "Traits")** are special features some Weapons and pieces of Equipment possess. They are all explained in the Labels and [Traits](/Traits "Traits") Section. An asterisk (\*) in this section indicates that the Weapon or piece of Equipment has extra rules in the corresponding section of the rulebook.

  

Example 2

A weapon that uses [AP](/AP "AP")+[DA](/DA "DA") Ammunition indicates that the target must perform 2 [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") for each hit received (due to DA Ammunition), with the corresponding Saving Roll Attribute halved (due to AP Ammunition).
