Some weapons in Infinity may require a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") against different Attributes.

These weapons will indicate the different combined Attributes with the plus sign ([ARM](/ARM "ARM")+[BTS](/BTS "BTS") for example).

When a [Critical](/Critical "Critical") is obtained with a weapon with a Combined Saving Roll, the target must make an additional Saving Roll against the ARM Attribute.

Examples of Combined Saving Rolls

A hit from a Plasma Carbine forces its target to make two [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Rolls "Saving Rolls"), one against the [ARM](/ARM "ARM") Attribute and the other against the [BTS](/BTS "BTS") Attribute. A [Critical](/Critical "Critical") from the Plasma Carbine forces its target to make an additional Saving Roll against the ARM Attribute. Therefore, the target will perform two Saving Rolls against the ARM Attribute and one against the BTS Attribute.
