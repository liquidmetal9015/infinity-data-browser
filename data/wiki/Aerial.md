Troopers possessing this Special Skill have flight capabilities. While in combat, Aerial Troopers stay as close to the ground as possible to avoid being an easy target.

AERIAL

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   **Restriction:** While this Skill is active, the Trooper cannot be in [Prone State](/Prone_State "Prone State") or [Engaged State](/Engaged_State "Engaged State").
*   **Restriction:** While this Skill is Active, the Trooper is never in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with other Models, Objectives, scenery items, or game elements, and vice versa, even if physically touching them.
*   **Restriction:** Troopers with this Special Skill cannot declare [Cautious Movement](/Cautious_Movement "Cautious Movement").

  

SEE ALSO

**[Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State")**  
Automatic Special Skills and Automatic Equipment have no effect while in Unconscious State.

  

IMPORTANT

**Aerial and Guard:**  

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  
The [Guard](/Guard "Guard") Special Skill may not be used against Troopers with the Aerial Skill, unless they are in [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious") State.

**Original**

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

![sibylla-n5.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/sibylla-n5.png)

Sibylla's Advice

The aerial movement of Troopers with this Skill is represented using the [Super-Jump](/Super-Jump "Super-Jump") and [Terrain](/Terrain "Terrain") (Total) Special Skills, which are listed in their [Unit Profiles](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile"). Similarly, the fact that these Troopers are harder to hit due to their higher speed and the height they can fly at is represented by [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism"), or other Special Skills.

In addition, it is impossible to enter [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with these Troopers unless they are in [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State"), so if they are not Unconscious any [Wounds](/Wounds "Wounds") they have received can only be removed by using a [GizmoKit](/GizmoKit "GizmoKit").

Sibylla's Advice

The aerial movement of Troopers with this Skill is represented using the [Super-Jump](/Super-Jump "Super-Jump") and [Terrain](/Terrain "Terrain") (Total) Special Skills, which are listed in their [Unit Profiles](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile"). Similarly, the fact that these Troopers are harder to hit due to their higher speed and the height they can fly at is represented by the [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism") or [Albedo](/Albedo "Albedo") Special Skills.

In addition, it is impossible to enter [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with these Troopers unless they are in [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State"), so if they are not Unconscious any [Wounds](/Wounds "Wounds") they have received can only be removed by using a [GizmoKit](/GizmoKit "GizmoKit").

**Q: Do Troopers with Aerial Special Skill activate Weapons with the Boost Trait?**

A: No, as it is not possible to enter [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with Aerial Troopers.
