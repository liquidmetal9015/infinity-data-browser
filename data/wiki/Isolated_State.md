ISOLATED STATE

ACTIVATION

*   The Trooper or game element ("Trooper" from now on) suffers a successful [Attack](/Attack "Attack") or Effect using an [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition"), or a game condition or Scenario Special Rule, capable of causing this state.

EFFECTS

*   **Restriction:** Troopers in Isolated State cannot receive [Orders](/Orders "Orders") from their [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool").
*   **Restriction:** If, at the start of their following [Active Turns](/Active_Turn "Active Turn"), the Trooper is still Isolated, then during that Turn they are [Irregular](/Irregular "Irregular") and do not add his Order to their Order Pool.
*   **Restriction:** The [Irregular Order](/Irregular_Order "Irregular Order") of an Isolated Trooper cannot be transformed in a [Regular Order](/Regular_Order "Regular Order").
*   **Restriction:** While in Isolated State all the Trooper's Skills and pieces of Equipment with the [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack") or [Comms Equipment](/Comms_Equipment "Comms Equipment") Labels or Traits ([Hacking Device](/Hacking_Device "Hacking Device"), [Repeater](/Repeater "Repeater")...) are disabled. In addition, all their [Hacking Programs](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") are disabled. Other Automatic Special Skills and Equipment continue to work in this state.
*   If the Isolated Trooper is the army's [Lieutenant](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant"), then at the beginning of the player's next Active Turn the army enters a [Loss of Lieutenant](/Loss_of_Lieutenant "Loss of Lieutenant") situation unless the state is canceled before then.
*   **Restriction** If a Trooper with the [NCO](/NCO "NCO") Special Skill is in Isolated State, they cannot replace [Special Lieutenant Orders](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order") with [Tactical Orders](/Tactical_Order "Tactical Order") or use them either.
*   If a [Peripheral](/Peripheral "Peripheral") or a Peripheral's Controller enters Isolated State, the Peripheral automatically enters [Disconnected State](/Disconnected_State "Disconnected State").
*   Troopers in this state must apply a -9 [WIP](/WIP "WIP") MOD to their [Reset](/Reset "Reset") rolls.
*   **Restriction:** A Trooper in Isolated State cannot be part of a [Fireteam](/Fireteam "Fireteam"). If a member of a Fireteam enters Isolated State they automatically stop being part of the Fireteam.  
    In addition, remember that if the Isolated Trooper is the Team Leader the Fireteam will break.
*   **Restriction:** Troopers in Isolated State cannot take part in a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order").

CANCELLATION

*   A Trooper with the [Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer") Special Skill (or an equivalent Skill) may cancel the state by spending one [Short Skill](/Short_Skill "Short Skill") of an [Order](/Trooper_Activation "Trooper Activation") while in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the affected Trooper and succeeding at a [Normal](/Normal "Normal") [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll (or the Roll specified by the Special Skill or Scenario that caused the state).
*   The affected Trooper may cancel this state via a successful [Normal](/Normal "Normal") or [Face to Face](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") [Reset](/Reset "Reset") Roll, applying the -9 WIP MOD provided by this state.
*   If the army is in a [Loss of Lieutenant](/Loss_of_Lieutenant "Loss of Lieutenant") situation as a result of the Lieutenant being in Isolated State, Canceling the Lieutenant's Isolated State does not cancel the Loss of Lieutenant situation.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Oblivion](/Oblivion "Oblivion")**

*   Any failed Saving Roll causes the target to enter Isolated State, placing an Isolated State Token (ISOLATED) by them.

  
**[Electromagnetic (E/M) Ammunition](/Electromagnetic_\(E/M\)_Ammunition "Electromagnetic (E/M) Ammunition")**

*   A failed Saving Roll against E/M Ammunition causes the target to enter Isolated State, placing an Isolated Token next to it, preventing it from receiving Orders from the [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool").
