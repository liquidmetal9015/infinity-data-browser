DEPLOYABLE REPEATER

EQUIPMENT

[Comms Equipment, Deployable, Disposable (3), Indiscriminate, Zone of Control.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   When the player declares the [Place Deployable](/Place_Deployable "Place Deployable") Common Skill, the Trooper places a Deployable Repeater Token (REPEATER) on the game table.
*   The Deployable Repeater is a piece of Equipment that contains a [Repeater](/Repeater "Repeater").

REMEMBER

[Firewall](/Firewall "Firewall") imposes an additional MOD (-3, -6...) to the WIP Roll of the Trooper declaring a [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack") against a Trooper benefitting from a Firewall, for example if attacking through an enemy [Repeater](/Repeater "Repeater").

The Trooper benefiting from the Firewall also applies a MOD of +3 to their [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") (SR) against the Comms Attack.

Only the MODs of a single Firewall can be applied, even if the Trooper benefits from more than one. In the case of the Trooper benefitting from more than one Firewall, their player will decide which one to apply.
