GUARD

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The target of the [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") must be in [LoF](/LoF "LoF") and [ZoC](/ZoC "ZoC").

EFFECTS

*   Allows the user to perform a CC Attack but with no need to reach or be in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the target.
*   Both the user and the target can perform Skills and apply MODs and CC Special Skills that require Silhouette Contact. For example: they can apply [Martial Arts](/Martial_Arts "Martial Arts"), [Natural Born Warrior](/Natural_Born_Warrior "Natural Born Warrior"), [D-Charges](/D-Charges "D-Charges"), or the [+1B MOD for each Allied Trooper engaged in CC](/Close_Combat_with_Multiple_Troopers "Close Combat with Multiple Troopers").
