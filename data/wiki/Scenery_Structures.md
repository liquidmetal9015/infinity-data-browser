Access Width (AW)
-----------------

In Infinity, the scenery used on the table to simulate the game and mission settings is usually made up of buildings with different kinds of access points, such as a doorway, a window, etc.

This section describes the different types of access points regarding their size and which Troopers are allowed to pass through them. There are two possible Access Widths:

*   Narrow. This access point is only big enough for Troopers with a [Silhouette](/Silhouette "Silhouette") Attribute of 2 or lower. It is marked by a Narrow Gate Token (NARROW GATE).
*   Wide. This access point allows all Troopers to pass through, regardless of their Silhouette value. It is marked by a Wide Gate Token (WIDE GATE).

Both players must agree on the Access Width the pieces of scenery could have before the game starts, marking them accordingly.

  

REMEMBER

Troopers in [Prone State](/Prone_State "Prone State"), with [Silhouette](/Silhouette "Silhouette") X, must apply the Silhouette Attribute value shown on their [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile") when considering restrictions for access points.

  

IMPORTANT

The Access Width rule always takes priority over the [General Movement Rules](/General_Movement_Rules "General Movement Rules") and the Requirements of Skills with the [Movement Label](/Movement_Label "Movement Label"). If a Trooper does not have the [Silhouette](/Silhouette "Silhouette") Attribute a specific access requires, the Trooper will not be able to go through it.

In Infinity, gates and accesses are always considered to be open, not blocking [Line of Fire](/Line_of_Fire "Line of Fire"), unless a special rule or scenario condition says otherwise.
