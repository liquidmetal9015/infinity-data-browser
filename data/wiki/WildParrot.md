This mobile weapons platform has been designed to be deployed on the battlefield at a distance, acting as a Mine with E/M Special Ammunition.

WILDPARROT

EFFECTS

*   These weapons are placed with the [Place Deployable](/Place_Deployable "Place Deployable") or [Intuitive Attack](/Intuitive_Attack "Intuitive Attack") Common Skills, always applying the [Deployable and Perimeter](/Place_Deployable#Deployable_and_Perimeter "Place Deployable") rule.
*   Therefore, when players deploy this weapon, they place the WildParrot totally inside the [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") of the Trooper, instead of placing it in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact").
*   Deployed WildParrots work like E/M [Mines](/Mines "Mines"), except that a WildParrot Token or Model is placed instead of a Camouflage Marker.

  

SEE ALSO

Once deployed, WildParrots work like **E/M [Mines](/Mines "Mines")**.
