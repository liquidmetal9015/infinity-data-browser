This piece of Equipment was designed to combat the effects of different military concealment and camouflage technologies.

MULTISPECTRAL VISOR LEVEL 1

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   This Visor reduces the MODs of [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism") (-3), and of [Low Visibility Zones](/Low_Visibility_Zone "Low Visibility Zone"), to 0.
*   This Visor reduces the MODs of Mimetism (-6), and of [Poor Visibility Zones](/Poor_Visibility_Zone "Poor Visibility Zone"), to -3.
*   Additionally, it allows the user to draw LoF through [Zero Visibility Zones](/Zero_Visibility_Zone "Zero Visibility Zone"), applying a -6 MOD to any Skill that requires [LoF](/LoF "LoF"). _[(See FAQs.)](#FAQs)_
*   Troopers equipped with this piece of Equipment do not make [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") against weapons using [Smoke Ammunition](/Smoke_Ammunition "Smoke Ammunition").

  

MULTISPECTRAL VISOR LEVEL 2

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   This Visor reduces MODs from [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism") and from [Visibility Zones](/Visibility_Zone "Visibility Zone") to 0.
*   In addition, it allows its user to draw [LoF](/LoF "LoF") through Visibility Zones without having to apply that Zone's MODs **to any Skill that requires [LoF](/LoF "LoF")**.
*   Troopers equipped with this piece of Equipment do not make [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") against weapons that use [Smoke Ammunition](/Smoke_Ammunition "Smoke Ammunition").

  

MULTISPECTRAL VISOR LEVEL 3

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   This Visor reduces MODs from [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism"), and from [Visibility Zones](/Visibility_Zone "Visibility Zone"), to 0.
*   In addition, it allows its user to draw [LoF](/LoF "LoF") through Visibility Zones without having to apply that Zone's MODs **to any Skill that requires [LoF](/LoF "LoF")**.
*   Troopers equipped with this piece of Equipment do not make [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") against weapons that use [Smoke Ammunition](/Smoke_Ammunition "Smoke Ammunition").
*   When performing [Discover](/Discover "Discover") against a target in [Camouflaged State](/Camouflaged_State "Camouflaged State"), the user's [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll is automatically a success.
*   Users of a Multispectral Visor L3 ignore [Surprise Attack](/Surprise_Attack "Surprise Attack") MODs if they have [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to the attacker. In addition, they also ignore Surprise Attack MODs from [CC Attacks](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") even if they don't have LoF to the attacker.
*   Additionally, in the [Active Turn](/Active_Turn "Active Turn") the user may perform a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") on a Camouflage Marker (CAMO) in LoF without making a previous Discover Roll, but they must apply the Mimetism MOD indicated on the Marker. However, the Cancellation clauses for Camouflaged State are applied as usual.

**Q: How do Sixth Sense, Zero Visibility Zones and Multipectral Visor Level 1 interact?**

A: If the Trooper with Multispectral Visor Level 1 and Sixth Sense is the target of a BS Attack through a Zero Visibility Zone, they ignore the -6 MOD for drawing LoF through the Zero Visibility Zone.
