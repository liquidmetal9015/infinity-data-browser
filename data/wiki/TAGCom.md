The TAGCom is the tactical uplink of the TAGs. It is their operational support and their eyes and ears on the ground, so it improves their survivability.

EFFECTS

*   While the user is deployed on the table in a non-[Null](/Null "Null") State, all [TAGs](/TAG "TAG") in their [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group") benefit from the MODs listed between brackets in the user's [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile").
*   Individual TAGCom MODs are **not** cumulative, the player may only apply those that are different from each other.
