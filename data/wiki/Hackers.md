The term Hacker designates a set of Troopers who have received specific training in Infowar. This training allows them to make use of Hacking Devices, and the programs specifically designed for them.

HACKER

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   Hackers may be equipped with a [Hacking Device](/Hacking_Device "Hacking Device") that will grant them access to certain [Hacking Programs](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program"), depending on the type of Device they are using, or to make direct use of certain Programs without requiring a device.
*   Hackers may have [Upgrade Programs](/Quantronic_Combat_\(Hacking\)#Upgrade_Programs_.28UPGRADE.29 "Quantronic Combat (Hacking)") assigned to them.
*   This Special Skill remains active even when its owner is in a [Null](/Null "Null") State.
