Users of Bangbombs often throw them at their own feet while dodging enemy fire, because, when the Bangbombs hit the ground and detonate, they create a curtain of reflective particles that enhance the flash of the blast, making it easier for the user to dodge and evade enemy attacks.

BANGBOMB

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user declares a [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge").

EFFECTS

*   The user applies a MOD to their Dodge Rolls. This MOD is listed in brackets after the name of this piece of Equipment in the user's [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile").
*   **Restriction:** This Dodge MOD **only** applies against Attacks declared by enemy Troopers who are within the user's [LoF](/LoF "LoF") or [ZoC](/ZoC "ZoC").
*   **Restriction:** This Dodge MOD does **not** apply against Attacks made with [Template Weapons](/Template_Weapon "Template Weapon").

  

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

Bangbomb Example

![eng-bangbomb-example.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/eng-bangbomb-example.jpg)

During their Active Turn, the player activates a Trooper with BangBomb (+4), declaring [Move](/Move "Move") as the first Skill of the Order. Two enemy Troopers in [LoF](/LoF "LoF") declare their [AROs](/ARO "ARO"), both [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack"), one with a Combi Rifle and the other with an E/Marat, a [Template Weapon](/Template_Weapon "Template Weapon").

The Active Trooper then declares [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") as the second Skill of the Order. Thanks to BangBomb (+4), they have a MOD of +4 to their Dodge Roll against the enemy with the Combi Rifle. Since the Active Trooper has PH 13, their [Success Value](/Success_Value "Success Value") is 17 (13+4). As the BangBomb MOD does not apply against Attacks with Template Weapons, their Success Value to Dodge the Template is 13.

Dice are rolled, and the Active Trooper gets a 14. This is enough to win the [Face to Face](/Face_to_Face "Face to Face") Roll against the enemy with a Combi Rifle, who rolls a 9.

However, the E/Marat Template hits the Active Trooper. As the Bangbomb MOD does not apply against Template Weapons, the Trooper would have needed to roll a 13 or less.

By failing to Dodge the Template, the Trooper is unable to move up to 2 inches, even though he succeeded against the Combi Rifle Attack.
