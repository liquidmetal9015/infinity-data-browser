This device is capable of deactivating traps and automated weapons deployed by the Enemy.

DEACTIVATOR

SHORT SKILL

[Attack](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The target must be within the user's [LoF](/LoF "LoF") or [ZoC](/ZoC "ZoC").
*   A Deactivator can only target enemy [Deployable Weapons](/Deployable_Weapon "Deployable Weapon") and Deployable pieces of Equipment that have been deployed on the game table, but never [Camouflage Markers](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged").

EFFECTS

*   The user of Deactivator, by passing a [Normal](/Normal "Normal") [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll, may deactivate the enemy Deployable Weapon or Deployable piece of Equipment ([Mines](/Mines "Mines"), [FastPandas](/FastPanda "FastPanda"), [WildParrots](/WildParrot "WildParrot"), [Deployable Cover](/Deployable_Cover "Deployable Cover"), [Deployable Repeaters](/Deployable_Repeater "Deployable Repeater"), [Armed Turrets](/Armed_Turret "Armed Turret"), etc.).
*   **Restriction:** Passing the WIP Roll does not prevent the enemy Deployable Weapon or Deployable piece of Equipment from triggering during the current Order.
*   The WIP Roll only applies MODs for [Range](/Range "Range"), but not those from Special Skills (for example [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism")), or [Cover](/Cover "Cover").
*   If the user passes the WIP Roll, the enemy Deployable weapon or Deployable piece of Equipment is removed from the game table.
