Some Troopers, equipped with special jump gear, have been trained to dive from the sky directly onto the battlefield, falling upon the enemy in devastating surprise strikes.

COMBAT JUMP

LONG SKILL

[Airborne Deployment (AD), Private Information, Optional.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   This Trooper is not required to deploy during the [Deployment Phase](/Deployment_Phase "Deployment Phase"), remaining off-table until their player decides to deploy them during their Active Turn.
*   While off-table, the Trooper will not add their Order to the [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool") during the [Tactical Phase](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase"). However, they may enter the table using their own Order to use this Special Skill, when the player chooses.
*   By using this Special Skill, the player cannot place the Trooper in [Prone State](/Prone_State "Prone State"), or in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with [enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") and [neutral](/Neutral "Neutral") Models, Markers, or Tokens, or in contact with any scenario objective.
*   The player may deploy the Trooper on any horizontal surface of the game table, as long as the size of the surface is equal to or larger than their base.
*   Deploying inside buildings or enclosed pieces of Scenery is not permitted, even if they have an open roof or open doors or windows like, for example, an Objective Room.
*   After placing the Trooper on their landing spot, with any [Deployable Weapons](/Deployable_Weapon "Deployable Weapon") or Equipment that they can deploy, the player performs a PH Roll. If the Roll is passed, the Trooper lands successfully and remains where the player has placed them. Then, the Reactive Player declares all their [AROs](/ARO "ARO").
*   If the user fails the Roll, they must be placed within their player's [Deployment Zone](/Deployment_Zone "Deployment Zone"), always in contact with the edge of the game table. Additionally, after failing the Roll, the user loses the option to deploy in a Marker State or as a [Decoy](/Decoy "Decoy") and is always deployed as a Model. Any Deployable Weapons and Equipment deployed alongside him are removed from the game table. Then, the Reactive Player declares all their AROs.
*   **Restriction**: During the Order this Special Skill is used, the Trooper cannot benefit from [Partial Cover](/Partial_Cover "Partial Cover").
*   When a player moves the Trooper into a different [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group"), while it is off the table, they must inform their opponent that the Trooper exists. The Trooper's information remains [Private](/Private "Private"), until the Trooper is deployed.
*   This Special Skill can be used during the [Impetuous Phase](/Impetuous_Phase "Impetuous Phase"), which requires removing both the Trooper's Impetuous Token and their own Order.
*   While off-table, Troopers with this Special Skill can be selected to deploy as part of a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order"). However, in this situation they will lose their own Order.

### Combat Jump (Explosion)

*   **Combat Jump (Explosion)** has the following additional rules:
*   After performing the PH Roll and determining the Trooper's final location, the Trooper makes a [Direct Template](/Direct_Template "Direct Template") Attack, placing the center of the Circular Template (Blast Focus) over the center of their base.
*   This Attack has PS 8 with Normal Ammunition, against the ARM Attribute.
*   The Trooper is not affected by this Attack.
*   This is a single-use Special Skill, and its user cannot perform it again.

  

REMEMBER

Troopers cannot be deployed in contact with [enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") and [neutral](/Neutral "Neutral") Models, Markers, or Tokens, or in contact with any mission objective, no matter the Special Skills they have.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Controlled Jump](/Controlled_Jump "Controlled Jump")**

*   As long as Controlled Jump is active, when performing the Combat Jump Skill every Allied Trooper applies a +3 MOD while every Enemy Trooper must apply a -3 MOD.

  
**[Visibility Conditions](/Visibility_Conditions#Visibility_Conditions.23Visibility_Conditions_and_Combat_Jump "Visibility Conditions")**

You cannot use Combat Jump inside of, or in contact with, an area with Low, Poor, or Zero Visibility Conditions.
