SPOTLIGHT

SHORT SKILL / ARO

[Comms Attack, Non-Lethal.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   In the Active Turn, this Program's [Burst](/Burst "Burst") of 2 allows the user to perform two [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Rolls, which can be distributed among one or more targets as usual.
*   Each successful Roll, due to [AP Ammunition](/AP_Ammunition "AP Ammunition"), forces the target to make a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") against their halved [BTS](/BTS "BTS"), with PS 5.
*   Any failed Saving Roll causes the target to enter [Targeted State](/Targeted_State "Targeted State"), placing a Targeted State Token (TARGETED) by them.
*   A [Critical](/Critical "Critical") with Spotlight forces its target to perform an additional Saving Roll, applying their halved BTS value.
*   The range of this Program is the [Hacker](/Hacker "Hacker")'s [Hacking Area](/Hacking_Area "Hacking Area").

  

IMPORTANT

The target of this [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") does not need to have the [Hackable](/Hackable "Hackable") Characteristic.

  

REMEMBER

The target of a [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") or a [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack") can declare a [Reset](/Reset "Reset"), regardless of their [Troop Type](/Troop_Type "Troop Type") (LI, MI, HI, etc.), and even if this [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") or [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack") is performed from outside their [LoF](/LoF "LoF").
