An Albedo jams Multispectral Visors and targeting systems, preventing its bearer from being the target of an Enemy Trooper carrying that piece of Equipment.

EFFECTS

*   Any enemy with a **[Multispectral Visor](/Multispectral_Visor "Multispectral Visor")** or the **[Marksmanship](/Marksmanship "Marksmanship")** Special Skill who declares a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") requiring [LoF](/LoF "LoF"), or [Discover](/Discover "Discover"), against the bearer of this piece of Equipment must apply a negative MOD to their Attribute, as shown in round brackets in the Unit Profile : Albedo (-3), Albedo (-6)...
*   The Albedo MOD does **not** apply to [CC Attacks](/CC_Attacks "CC Attacks").
