This piece of Equipment has been conceived to reduce the effects of the different impersonation and disguise technologies.

BIOMETRIC VISOR

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   The user ignores the [WIP](/WIP "WIP")\-3 MOD imposed by the [Impersonation-1 State](/Impersonation_State "Impersonation State").
*   If the user passes a [Discover](/Discover "Discover") Roll against a Trooper in Impersonation-1 State, that Trooper's Impersonation State is canceled and their IMP-1 Marker is replaced with the corresponding Model.
*   Users of a Biometric Visor ignore [Surprise Attack](/Surprise_Attack "Surprise Attack") MODs from attackers benefitting from [Impersonation](/Impersonation_State "Impersonation State") or [Holoecho States](/Holoecho_State "Holoecho State"), if they have [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to the attacker. In addition, they also ignore Surprise Attack MODs from [CC Attacks](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") from those attackers even if they don't have LoF to the attacker.
