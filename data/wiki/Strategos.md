This Special Skill identifies professional strategists, individuals with superior analytical minds and a wealth of knowledge on the art of war and its psychological implications.

STRATEGOS L1

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   In order to benefit from this Level of Strategos, the user must be the army's [Lieutenant](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant").

EFFECTS

*   During the [Deployment Phase](/Deployment_Phase "Deployment Phase"), you may set aside two [Troopers](/Trooper "Trooper") and their [Peripherals](/Peripheral "Peripheral") to deploy after your opponent instead of the usual one.
*   At the beginning of your Active Turn, during your [Order Count](/Order_Count "Order Count"), replace any [Special Lieutenant Orders](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order") with [Regular Orders](/Regular_Order "Regular Order"), adding them to the [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool") of the Lieutenant's [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group").

  

STRATEGOS L2

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   In order to benefit from this Level of Strategos, the user must be the army's [Lieutenant](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant").

EFFECTS

*   During the [Tactical Phase](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase") of the Active Turn, in the [Executive Use of Command Tokens](/Executive_Use_of_Command_Tokens "Executive Use of Command Tokens") step, the user of this Special Skill may move a Trooper to a different [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group") without expending a [Command Token](/Command_Token "Command Token").
*   During the [Deployment Phase](/Deployment_Phase "Deployment Phase"), you may set aside two [Troopers](/Trooper "Trooper") and their [Peripherals](/Peripheral "Peripheral") to deploy after your opponent instead of the usual one.
*   At the beginning of your Active Turn, during your [Order Count](/Order_Count "Order Count"), replace any [Special Lieutenant Orders](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order") with [Regular Orders](/Regular_Order "Regular Order"), adding them to the [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool") of the Lieutenant's Combat Group.
