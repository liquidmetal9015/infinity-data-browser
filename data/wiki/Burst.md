In Infinity, combat allows Troopers to perform Attacks. There are three kinds of [Attacks](/Attacks "Attacks"): Ballistic Skills (BS), Close Combat (CC) and Quantronic Combat (Hacking). This section details every rule necessary to resolve Attacks.

[BS](/Ballistic_Skills "Ballistic Skills"), [CC](/Close_Combat "Close Combat"), or [Hacking](/Hacking "Hacking") combat takes place within the normal [Order Expenditure Sequence](/Order_Expenditure_Sequence "Order Expenditure Sequence") and it is based on the different Rolls in Infinity.

Types of Weapon
---------------

Each weapon in Infinity has its own game profile, and some of them have special rules that are explained in the [Ammunition and Weaponry](/Ammunition_and_Weaponry "Ammunition and Weaponry") chapter. This rulebook also contains a [Weapons Chart](/Weapons_Chart "Weapons Chart") for you to refer to during games.

Weapons in Infinity are divided as follows:

*   **[BS Weapons](/BS_Weapon "BS Weapon")**: Those weapons having Ranges and Range MODs or using a Template to affect an area of the game table.
*   **[Melee Weapons](/Melee_Weapon "Melee Weapon")**: Close-quarters weapons and those that can only be used in CC and possess the [CC Trait](/CC_Trait "CC Trait").
*   **[Mixed Weapons](/Mixed_Weapons "Mixed Weapons")**: Those weapons having Ranges and Range MODs but also the CC Trait. So, they have a Use Mode as a Melee Weapons and another Use Mode as a BS Weapon
*   **[Deployable Weapons](/Deployable_Weapon "Deployable Weapon")**: Those weapons that can be placed on the game table whereupon they become an independent element. They possess the [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Weapon Trait.
*   **[BS Weapons (PH)](/BS_Weapon_\(PH\) "BS Weapon (PH)")**: These weapons are BS Weapons but are used with the [PH](/PH "PH") Attribute instead of [BS](/BS "BS"). When using these weapons, any rules or MODs that apply to BS Attacks or to the BS Attribute are applied to the PH Attribute instead. These weapons have the BS Weapons (PH) Trait.
*   **[BS Weapons (WIP)](/BS_Weapon_\(WIP\) "BS Weapon (WIP)")**: These weapons are BS Weapons but are used with the [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Attribute instead of [BS](/BS "BS"). When using these weapons, any rules or MODs that apply to BS Attacks or to the BS Attribute are applied to the WIP Attribute instead. These weapons have the BS Weapons (WIP) Trait. The BS Attack ([Shock](/Shock "Shock")) Skill cannot be combined with these weapons.

Burst (B)
---------

In Infinity, when a Trooper performs an Attack during their Active Turn they must roll as many dice as indicated by their Weapon, Skill or piece of Equipment.

During their Active Turn, Troopers must use their **full Burst (B) value** including all Modifiers (MODs). However, MODs from Skills and Equipment with the [Optional](/Optional "Optional") Label, such as the [Fireteam Burst Bonus](/Fireteam_Bonuses "Fireteam Bonuses"), do not have to be applied during the Active or Reactive Turn.

When declaring the Attack, the player must specify:

*   The [Weapon](/Weapon "Weapon"), [Equipment](/Equipment "Equipment"), or [Special Skill](/Special_Skill "Special Skill") that they will use.
*   The way they will divide the Burst (B) of the Weapon, Equipment, or Special Skill between one or more targets. In this situation, all the Attacks must be declared from the same point.
*   Additionally, if the Weapon, Equipment, or Special Skill has different types of [ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition") or options, the player must then declare which one they will use.

If, during the [Resolution](/Resolution "Resolution") of the Order, it is verified that some of the dice of the Burst (B) assigned to a target do not meet the necessary Requirements, then those dice will be lost. The rest of the dice of the B that do meet the Requirements will be resolved normally.

  

Burst During the Reactive Turn (ARO)
------------------------------------

In [ARO](/ARO "ARO"), the B value is always reduced to 1; however, some rules or Special Skills can modify this value. If the target declared [Move](/Move "Move") plus any Short Skill or Basic Short Skill, the Reactive Player may declare the ARO at any point along the Active Trooper’s movement.

  

Modifiers (MOD)
---------------

Both during the Active and Reactive Turns, there may be Modifiers ([MODs](/MODs "MODs")) that affect the Burst (B). They are applied when declaring the Attack. There are also MODs to the Attack Rolls, which are applied in the [Resolution](/Resolution "Resolution") step of the Order. These MODs are explained in the sections for their respective Types of Combat ([BS](/Ballistic_Skills "Ballistic Skills"), [CC](/Close_Combat "Close Combat"), or [Hacking](/Hacking "Hacking")).

  

SEE ALSO

**[Modifiers Explained](/Modifiers_Explained "Modifiers Explained")**

Any MOD or value in round brackets next to a [Special Skill](/Special_Skill "Special Skill"),[Weapon](/Weapon "Weapon"), or [Equipment](/Equipment "Equipment")—such as [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") (+3), [Combat Jump](/Combat_Jump "Combat Jump") (PH=10), [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") (+3), [CC Weapon](/CC_Weapon "CC Weapon") (PS=6), Combi Rifle (+1B), etc.—**only applies when using** the Skill, Weapon, or Equipment.

*   **Positive MODs** only apply to the **user**.
*   **Negative MODs** only apply to **enemies**.
    *   Negative MODs for Automatic Skills and Equipment such as [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism") (-6), or [Surprise Attack](/Surprise_Attack "Surprise Attack") (-3) always apply the MOD as specified in their rules.
    *   Negative MODs for other Skills, Weapons, and Equipment such as [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") (-3), or [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") (-3) only apply during [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll").
*   "**ReRoll**" in round brackets next to a Skill, Weapon, or Equipment will allow the user to reroll one die from the Roll, only when using that Skill, Weapon, or Equipment.
*   **"+1 SD"** or "+1 Special Die" in round brackets next to a Skill, Weapon, or Equipment allows the player to roll one extra die, and then select and discard one die from the Roll. This extra die does not increase the Burst value of the Special Skill, Weapon, or Equipment, and does not expend additional Disposable uses. If distributing Burst between several targets, the extra die must be allocated to one of those targets, and a die discarded from the Roll against that target.
    *   If used in a [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll"), a die is selected and discarded after both players have rolled. If both players have to select and discard a die, the Active Player selects first.
    *   This MOD applies in both Active and Reactive Turns.
    *   This MOD cannot be applied to [Long Skills](/Long_Skill "Long Skill"). Also, it cannot be applied to Skills, Weapons, or Equipment that do not require a Roll, for example, [Direct Template Weapons](/Direct_Template_Weapon "Direct Template Weapon").

  

IMPORTANT

As a general rule, if these Modifiers (MODs) affect [Burst](/Burst "Burst") (B), they are only applied during the Active Turn.

The maximum Burst (B) value of any Attack is 6, no matter how many MODs to B are applied. **The extra dice for "+1SD" do not count towards this maximum, as they do not increase the Burst value.**

  

Modifier Examples

MODs imposed by Skills, Special Skills, or pieces of Equipment may be applied to their users, or to those enemy Troopers acting against them, in several different ways. For example:

**[BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") (+1B)** means that, when its user declares a BS Attack in their [Active Turn](/Active_Turn "Active Turn"), they must apply a +1 MOD to their BS Weapon's [Burst](/Burst "Burst"). They may not, however, apply this MOD during their [Reactive Turn](/Reactive_Turn "Reactive Turn") if they declare a BS Attack [ARO](/ARO "ARO").

**[BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") (Shock)** means that the user adds [Shock](/Shock "Shock") Ammunition to all their BS Attacks.

**[Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism") (-3)** means that a Trooper declaring [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") or [Discover](/Discover "Discover") against the user must apply an additional -3 MOD to their Roll.

**[Combat Jump](/Combat_Jump "Combat Jump") (PH=10)** means that, when performing the [PH](/PH "PH") Roll required by this Skill, a PH value of 10 must be applied instead of the user's PH Attribute.

**[ECM](/ECM "ECM") (Hacking -3)** means that a Trooper declaring a [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") against the user must apply an additional -3 MOD to their Roll.

**[Immunity](/Immunity "Immunity") (POS)** means that the user cannot enter the [Possessed State](/Possessed_State "Possessed State").

**Heavy Rocket Launcher (PS=5)** means that the [PS](/PS "PS") of this Weapon is 5 in all usage Modes, regardless of the different PS values listed in the Weapons Chart for its Modes.

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

**[Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") (-3)** means that, when the user declares Dodge, the opponents in any [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") suffer a -3 MOD.

**[BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") (Continuous Damage)** means that the user applies the [Continuous Damage](/Continuous_Damage "Continuous Damage") Trait to all their BS Attacks.

**[BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") (SR-1)** means that, when the user declares BS Attack, their targets apply a -1 MOD to their [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") against the user’s hits.

  

Attack Roll
-----------

Once all MODs have been established, players perform the [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack"), [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), or [Hacking](/Hacking "Hacking") Rolls using the corresponding [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute") which, as a general—but not exclusive—rule, is [BS](/BS "BS"), [CC](/CC "CC"), [PH](/PH "PH"), or [WIP](/WIP "WIP"). The order in which the Attack Rolls are performed is irrelevant.

  

Normal Roll
-----------

If the Skill declared by the target does not affect the outcome of the Attack, the attacker can make a [Normal Roll](/Normal_Roll "Normal Roll") (see [Rolls](/Rolls "Rolls")).

The player performs as many Rolls as indicated by the Burst (B) value assigned to each target.

  

Face to Face Roll
-----------------

If the Skill declared by the target does affect the outcome of the Attack (for example declaring a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack")), then a [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") is made (see [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Rolls "Face to Face Rolls")).

Both players make one Roll for each point of Burst they assigned to that target.

  

Criticals
---------

A [Critical](/Critical "Critical") is an automatic success. Unless otherwise specified, each Critical rolled in an Attack causes the target to make an additional [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll").

If the Attack does not cause the target to make Saving Rolls, the effect of the Critical will be specified in the rules for that Attack, [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition") etc.

  

NOTE

Some weapons use certain Types of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition") capable of altering the effects of an Attack, causing more than one [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") for each success, reducing the [ARM](/ARM "ARM") or [BTS](/BTS "BTS") of the target, etc. This can also alter the way Criticals work.

The additional roll gained by scoring a [Critical](/Critical "Critical") Hit will retain both the [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute") used to make the Saving Roll and the [Traits](/Traits "Traits") of the weapon used in the attack.

  

REMEMBER

In a [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll"), [Criticals](/Critical "Critical") always win, trumping any non-critical result rolled by the opponent. If both players roll one or more Criticals, the Face to Face Roll is a tie and both Troopers fail.
