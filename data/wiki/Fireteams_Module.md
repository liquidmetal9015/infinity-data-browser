The Infinity Fireteams rule enables a team of Troopers to move and act as one in support of its **Fireteam Leader**, using fewer Orders than usual.

  

![sibylla-n5.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/sibylla-n5.png)

Sibylla's Advice

This rule allows the activation of groups composed of 2 to 5 Troopers by spending a single [Regular Order](/Regular_Order "Regular Order") during the [Active Turn](/Active_Turn "Active Turn"), which will grant a set of [Bonuses](/Fireteam_Bonuses "Fireteam Bonuses") in both the Active and [Reactive Turn](/Reactive_Turn "Reactive Turn").

These Troopers must be in [Coherency](/Coherency "Coherency") by remaining inside the [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") of the Fireteam Leader, the Trooper who bears the weight of the Order during the Active Turn. Fireteam Leadership can alternate between the Fireteam members; players can change the Leader with each new [Order](/Trooper_Activation "Trooper Activation").

  

IMPORTANT

The following Troopers cannot be part of a Fireteam:

*   [Peripherals](/Peripheral "Peripheral") and their Controllers.
*   Troopers in [Marker](/Marker "Marker") form.
*   Troopers with the [Infiltration](/Infiltration "Infiltration") Special Skill.
*   Troopers possessing Skills with the [Airborne Deployment](/Airborne_Deployment "Airborne Deployment") Label.
*   Troopers that activate or are in the [Suppressive Fire State](/Suppressive_Fire_State "Suppressive Fire State").
*   Troopers who deploy in [Decoy State](/Decoy_State "Decoy State").
*   Troopers in [Isolated State](/Isolated_State "Isolated State") or any [Null](/Null "Null") State.

Additionally, members of a Fireteam cannot be part of a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order").
