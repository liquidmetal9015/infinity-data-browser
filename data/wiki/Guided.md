Common Skill employed to attack in ranged combat using a BS Weapon or another suitable weapon or piece of Equipment.

BS ATTACK

SHORT SKILL /ARO

[Attack.](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   Be using a [BS Weapon](/BS_Weapon "BS Weapon"), or a [Skill](/Skill "Skill") or piece of [Equipment](/Equipment "Equipment") capable of making a BS Attack.
*   Be able to draw [Line of Fire](/Line_of_Fire "Line of Fire") ([LoF](/LoF "LoF")) from the position of your Attack to the position of the target, unless the BS Weapon, Skill or piece of Equipment used doesn't require LoF.

EFFECTS

*   The user employs his [BS](/BS "BS") [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute") (or Attribute specified by the weapon) to fire upon one or more enemies.
*   When declaring a BS Attack, the user must choose which of his BS Weapons, Special Skills or pieces of Equipment to use, from those available in his [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile").
*   If the attacker has more than one target and a BS Weapon, Special Skill or piece of Equipment with a [Burst](/Burst "Burst") value higher than 1, they must distribute their attacks as part of the BS Attack declaration.
*   All shots must be declared **from the same point**.
*   If the chosen weapon has more than one Type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition") available, you must choose one for the entire Burst, as part of the BS Attack declaration.

### BS Attack (Guided)

*   Troopers with the **BS Attack (Guided)** Skill listed on their [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile"):
    *   During the Active Turn, they may perform a BS Attack against a target in [Targeted State](/Targeted_State "Targeted State"), without requiring [LoF](/LoF "LoF").

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

*   *   BS Attacks (Guided) must be carried out with the weapon's Blast Mode if it has one, or a Mode with the [Impact Template (Circular) Trait](/Traits#Impact_Template "Traits"). If the weapon does not have a Mode with the Impact Template (Circular) Trait, the player may choose any of the weapon's Modes but they will always apply the Circular Template Trait, placing a Circular Template centered on the target.
    *   BS Attacks (Guided) must be carried out with the weapon's Blast Mode if it has one, or a Mode with the [Impact Template (Circular) Trait](/Traits#Impact_Template "Traits"). If the weapon does not have a Mode with the Impact Template (Circular) Trait, the player may choose any of the weapon's Modes and apply the Trait to that Mode.
    *   A Trooper can avoid a BS Attack (Guided) with a successful [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") or [Reset](/Reset "Reset") [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll"). If a Reset Roll is successful, in addition to avoiding the Attack, the Trooper's Targeted State is cancelled.
    *   **Restriction:** Unless the Skill itself indicates otherwise, the B will always be 1 regardless of the actual B value of the Weapon or any MODs applied to the B.
    *   **Restriction:** The distance to the target cannot exceed the weapon's maximum [Range](/Range "Range"), applying MODs as usual.

**Original**  

*   *   BS Attacks (Guided) must be carried out with the weapon's Blast Mode if it has one, or a Mode with the [Impact Template (Circular) Trait](/Traits#Impact_Template "Traits"). If the weapon does not have a Mode with the Impact Template (Circular) Trait, the player may choose any of the weapon's Modes and apply the Trait to that Mode.
    *   BS Attacks (Guided) must be carried out with the weapon's Blast Mode if it has one, or a Mode with the [Impact Template (Circular) Trait](/Traits#Impact_Template "Traits"). If the weapon does not have a Mode with the Impact Template (Circular) Trait, the player may choose any of the weapon's Modes and apply the Trait to that Mode.
    *   A Trooper can avoid a BS Attack (Guided) with a successful [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") or [Reset](/Reset "Reset") [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll"). If a Reset Roll is successful, in addition to avoiding the Attack, the Trooper's Targeted State is cancelled.
    *   **Restriction:** Unless the Skill itself indicates otherwise, the B will always be 1 regardless of the actual B value of the Weapon or any MODs applied to the B.
    *   **Restriction:** The distance to the target cannot exceed the weapon's maximum [Range](/Range "Range").

*   *   **Restriction:** The target cannot be in an enclosed room; a possible trajectory for the Attack must always exist.
    *   **Restriction:** BS Attack (Guided) has a limit of 5 Attacks per Turn for the Active Player.
    *   **Restriction:** Weapons with the [BS Weapon (PH)](/BS_Weapon_\(PH\) "BS Weapon (PH)") or [BS Weapon (WIP)](/BS_Weapon_\(WIP\) "BS Weapon (WIP)") Traits cannot be used with the BS Attack (Guided) Skill.
    *   The user must apply any [Range](/Range "Range") MODs. Other negative MODs (for example [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism"), [Partial Cover](/Partial_Cover "Partial Cover"), [Visibility Zones](/Visibility_Zone "Visibility Zone")...) are not applied unless they specify that they work against BS Attack (Guided).

  

SEE ALSO

**[Modifiers Explained](/Modifiers_Explained "Modifiers Explained") Examples**

*   **BS Attack (-3)** means that, when the user declares BS Attack, a -3 MOD applies to the opponent in any [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Rolls "Face to Face Rolls"). BS Attack (-X) has no effect against Troopers with the [Warhorse](/Warhorse "Warhorse") Special Skill.
*   **BS Attack (SR-1)** means that, when the user declares BS Attack, their targets apply a -1 MOD to their [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Rolls "Saving Rolls") against the user's hits.
*   **BS Attack (Shock)** means that the user adds [Shock Ammunition](/Shock_Ammunition "Shock Ammunition") to all their BS Attacks.
*   **BS Attack (Continuous Damage)** means that the user applies the [Continuous Damage](/Continuous_Damage "Continuous Damage") Trait to all their BS Attacks.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Guts Roll](/Guts_Roll "Guts Roll")**  
When Troopers survive a successful Attack, it means their body armor took the brunt of the impact, keeping them from serious injury. This triggers a primal fear of death, and survival instincts kick in. The Troopers' survival instincts can force them to recoil from danger and dive for cover.

  
**[Alert](/Alert "Alert")**  
Troopers are aware of what is happening around them and are also connected to their comrades, so attacks do not go unnoticed.

  
**Ballistic Skills > [BS Attack Into a Close Combat](/BS_Attack_Into_a_Close_Combat "BS Attack Into a Close Combat")**  
If a BS Attack is declared against an enemy Trooper that is engaged in CC Combat a -6 MOD must be applied for each Allied Trooper engaged in the [CC](/Close_Combat "Close Combat") (in addition to any MODs for [Range](/Range "Range"), [Cover](/Cover "Cover"), [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism")... for the target).

Every failed BS Attack Roll will hit the Allied Trooper, forcing them to perform a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll"). If there are several Allied Troopers locked in that Close Combat, then the Trooper’s player chooses which one of them receives each hit.

  

REMEMBER

The target must apply a -3 MOD to [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") if they have no [LoF](/LoF "LoF") towards the Attacker.

The [Targeted State](/Targeted_State "Targeted State") gives the target a -3 MOD to [Reset](/Reset "Reset") Rolls, also any BS Attack against them gets a +3 MOD.

  

Move + BS Attack

Since the Skills used in an Order are simultaneous, a BS Attack can be declared at any point during the route followed by the [Move](/Move "Move") Skill. If the [Burst](/Burst "Burst") is divided among several targets, all shots must be made from a single point. Usually, the attack is made from the position that offers the biggest advantage: a clear [LoF](/LoF "LoF"), targets out of [Cover](/Cover "Cover"), optimal range, etc.

[Anti-materiel](/Anti-materiel "Anti-materiel")

The Missile Launcher Remote declares a BS Attack (Guided) against a Trooper with the [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism") (-6) Special Skill, who is in [Targeted State](/Targeted_State "Targeted State").

The following MODs apply to the Remote's BS Attack (Guided) Roll:

*   +3 for [Range](/Range "Range").
*   +3 for the target's Targeted State.

As the Remote is using BS Attack (Guided), they do not have to apply the target's Mimetism (-6) MOD, nor any MODs for [Cover](/Cover "Cover"). In addition, of the two firing modes available to the Missile Launcher, the Attack must be made with Blast Mode (EXP ammunition).

The target of the Attack has two ARO options:

*   [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") with a -3 MOD due to Dodging a Template Weapon without [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to the Attacker.
*   [Reset](/Reset "Reset") with a -3 MOD due to being in Targeted State.

Therefore, the REM makes a Face-to-Face BS Attack Roll (BS +6) against the target's Dodge Roll (PH -3) or Reset Roll (WIP -3) Roll.

**Q: Do Skills that let you perform a 'BS Attack Roll' (for example Intuitive Attack and Speculative Attack), or have the BS Attack Label, count as a BS Attack for MODs? For example, if the Trooper has 'BS Attack (SR-1)', 'BS Attack (+1 SD), or 'BS Attack (AP)'.**

A: Yes. Note that +1 SD MODs don't apply to Long Skills.
