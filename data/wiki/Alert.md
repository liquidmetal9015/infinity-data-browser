Troopers are aware of what is happening around them and are also connected to their comrades, so attacks do not go unnoticed.

ALERT!

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional, No Roll.](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The Trooper has not have been activated by an [Order](/Order "Order") or [ARO](/ARO "ARO") in the same Order.
*   The Trooper, or an [Allied](/Allied "Allied") Trooper within their [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control"), has been the target of an Attack.

EFFECTS

*   At the end of the Order, during its [Conclusion](/Conclusion "Conclusion"), and after performing any [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll"), Troopers that meet the Requirements may turn on the spot, without changing position, to modify their [LoF](/LoF "LoF") angle as their player chooses.
*   This movement is automatic, it does not require a Roll and does not generate [AROs](/ARO "ARO"), since it happens during the Conclusion Step of the Order.
