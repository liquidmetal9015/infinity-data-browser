Skill that allows [Close Combat](/Close_Combat "Close Combat") fighting, striking an adversary in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact").

CC ATTACK

SHORT SKILL / ARO

[Attack](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   Be using a [Melee Weapon](/Melee_Weapon_Profile "Melee Weapon Profile"), a [Skill](/Skill "Skill"), or piece of [Equipment](/Equipment "Equipment") that allows for a CC Attack.
*   Be in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with an [enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") [Model](/Model "Model") or [Target](/Target "Target").

EFFECTS

*   Allows use of the [CC](/CC "CC") [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute") to fight in Close Combat (CC).
*   By declaring a CC Attack, Troopers may only use those Weapons with the [CC Trait](/CC_Trait "CC Trait").
*   If a Trooper has several targets and a **[Burst](/Burst "Burst")** value that is higher than 1, the player must clarify how they are dividing the Burst when declaring the CC Attack.
*   In the [Resolution](/Resolution "Resolution") step, check which MODs to apply, and make the CC Attack Rolls using the corresponding Attribute.
*   For each success obtained in CC Attack Rolls, the target must perform a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") (see [Possibility of Survival (PS)](/Possibility_of_Survival_\(PS\) "Possibility of Survival (PS)")).

### Coup de Grâce

*   **Coup de Grâce**: When declaring the CC Attack, if the [Enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") [Target](/Target "Target") is in [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State") (including if it is represented by a Shasvastii-Embryo Token), then, without needing to perform a roll, the target automatically goes from Unconscious to [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State") without the possibility of making a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll").  
    Coup de Grâce cannot be used against a Trooper who has activated the **[Dogged](/Dogged "Dogged")** or **[No Wound Incapacitation](/No_Wound_Incapacitation "No Wound Incapacitation")** Special Skill. Therefore, the CC Attack is resolved normally.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Modifiers Explained](/Modifiers_Explained "Modifiers Explained") Examples**

*   **CC Attack (-3)** means that, when the user declares CC Attack, their targets apply a -3 MOD to any [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Rolls "Face to Face Rolls").
*   **CC Attack (SR-1)** means that, when the user declares CC Attack, their targets apply a -1 MOD to their [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Rolls "Saving Rolls") against the user's hits.
*   **CC Attack (Shock)** means that the user adds [Shock Ammunition](/Shock_Ammunition "Shock Ammunition") to all their CC Attacks.
*   **CC Attack (Continuous Damage)** means that the user applies the [Continuous Damage](/Continuous_Damage "Continuous Damage") Trait to all their CC Attacks.
*   **PARA CC Weapon (-3)** means that, when the CC Weapon is being used, the user's targets apply a -3 MOD to any [Face to Face Rolls](/Face_to_Face_Rolls "Face to Face Rolls").
