The Trooper's urge to kill is barely contained. Once he gets a first taste of blood, he will become a whirlwind of death.

FRENZY

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory, States Phase.](/Labels "Labels")

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

REQUIREMENTS

*   Directly inflict at least one [Wound](/Wound "Wound") on an [Enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") Trooper, or cause them to enter [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State").

EFFECTS

*   In the States Phase of the Player Turn where the Requirements have been met, the Trooper gains the following Special Skills and retains them for the remainder of the game:
    *   [Impetuous](/Impetuous "Impetuous")
    *   [Limited Cover](/Limited_Cover "Limited Cover")
*   When Frenzy Troopers gain the Impetuous Special Skill, they cancel any Marker State they are in ([Camouflaged](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged"), [Impersonation](/Impersonation_State "Impersonation State")...), and any other States that say so. These Troopers cannot re-enter those States.

Show original text.

**Original**  

ACTIVATION

*   Automatic in the [States Phase](/States_Phase "States Phase") if the user has inflicted at least one [Wound](/Wound "Wound") on an [Enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") Trooper, or caused them to enter [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State").

EFFECTS

*   If the user inflicts at least one Wound on an enemy Trooper, or causes them to enter Dead State, then in the next States Phase the Trooper gains the following Special Skills and retains them for the remainder of the game:
    *   [Impetuous](/Impetuous "Impetuous")
    *   [Limited Cover](/Limited_Cover "Limited Cover")
*   When Frenzy Troopers gain the Impetuous Special Skill, they cancel any Marker State they are in ([Camouflaged](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged"), [Impersonation](/Impersonation_State "Impersonation State")...), and any other States that say so. These Troopers cannot re-enter those States.

Sibylla's Advice

The Requirements for this Special Skill demand that the Frenzy Trooper must inflict the [Wound](/Wound "Wound") directly on the target, or cause them to enter Dead State. The Trooper’s [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Weapons are not taken into account for the fulfillment of the Requirements.
