This Special Skill indicates the possession of hybrid self-repairing equipment that can be treated by both combat medics and field engineers.

TECHNORGANIC

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The Trooper is in [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious") State.

EFFECTS

*   The Unconscious State of the Trooper can be cancelled by using either the [Doctor](/Doctor "Doctor") or [Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer") Skill, or the [MediKit](/MediKit "MediKit") or [GizmoKit](/GizmoKit "GizmoKit") pieces of Equipment, regardless of whether they have the [VITA](/VITA "VITA") or [STR](/STR "STR") Attribute.
*   This Special Skill remains active even when its owner is in a [Null](/Null "Null") State.

  

Example

A Trooper with a [STR](/STR "STR") Attribute of 2 and the Technorganic Skill has received two [Wounds](/Wound "Wound") and is in [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious") State.

If a Trooper with the [Doctor](/Doctor "Doctor") Special Skill reaches [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact"), they could use Doctor to cancel the target's Unconscious State and remove one of the target's Wounds, even though their target lacks the [VITA](/VITA "VITA") Attribute.

If the Trooper reaching Silhouette contact has the [Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer") Special Skill instead of Doctor, they could use Engineer to remove one of the target's Wounds and cancel their Unconscious State, and in a subsequent Order remove the remaining Wound, as is usual for targets with the STR Attribute.

If the Trooper in Unconscious State has a VITA Attribute of 2 instead of the STR Attribute, receives a successful hit from a [GizmoKit](/GizmoKit "GizmoKit") and passes the corresponding roll, they remove a Wound and therefore cancel their Unconscious State. However, a new successful hit from a GizmoKit―succeeding the corresponding roll―would have no effect, as the Trooper lacks the STR Attribute, and Technorganic requires the Trooper to be in Unconscious State.
