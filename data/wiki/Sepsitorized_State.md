SEPSITORIZED STATE

[Null](/Labels "Labels")

ACTIVATION

*   A [Cube](/Cube "Cube")\-wearing Trooper failed a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") against a [Sepsitor](/Sepsitor "Sepsitor") Attack.

EFFECTS

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

*   **Restriction:** If a Trooper enters Sepsitorized State they automatically stop being part of their [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group").

*   **Restriction:** Troopers in this state **cannot be activated or receive [Orders](/Orders "Orders") from their player's [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool")**. While they are Sepsitorized, Troopers are considered to be [enemies](/Enemy "Enemy") by the rest of their owner's Troopers and are considered to be [allies](/Allied "Allied") by the Troopers of the player that caused this game state.
*   **Restriction:** Troopers in this state do not contribute [Orders](/Orders "Orders") during the [Tactical Phases](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase") of either player.
*   Troopers in this state may be **activated and receive [Orders](/Orders "Orders") from the [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool") of the [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group") of the Trooper who caused them to enter the Sepsitorized state**. A Sepsitorized Trooper does not count towards the maximum number of members the Combat Group may contain.
*   This state does not interfere with Automatic Special Skills or Automatic Equipment, which will remain functioning normally.
*   **Restriction:** A Sepsitorized Trooper does not count towards either Players' [Victory Points](/Victory_Points "Victory Points").
*   **Restriction:** A Trooper in Sepsitorized State cannot be part of a [Fireteam](/Fireteam "Fireteam"). If a member of a Fireteam enters Sepsitorized State, they automatically stop being part of the Fireteam.
