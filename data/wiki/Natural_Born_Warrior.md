Troopers with this CC Special Skill are superb fighters, able to cancel the negative MODs that their enemies try to impose on them in Close Combat.

NATURAL BORN WARRIOR

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[CC Special Skill](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user must be the target of a [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") and must declare a [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack").

EFFECTS

*   In the CC [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll"), the user ignores all negative MODs imposed by the opposing Trooper.

  

![sibylla-n5.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/sibylla-n5.png)

Sibylla's Advice

A Natural Born Warrior who is the target of a [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") from a Trooper with [Surprise Attack](/Surprise_Attack "Surprise Attack") (-3), [Martial Arts](/Martial_Arts "Martial Arts") Level 3, CC Attack (-3), and PARA CC Weapon (-3) and declares CC Attack themselves does not suffer any of the negative MODs normally caused by these Skills and Weapons in the [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll").
