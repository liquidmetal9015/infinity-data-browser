The user of this Special Skill can accomplish missions and objectives when playing scenarios, even if he does not have one of the roles specified as a Specialist Troop in the scenario.

SPECIALIST OPERATIVE

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   Playing a mission or a scenario, the user of this Special Skill is considered to be a [Specialist Troop](/Specialist_Troop "Specialist Troop") and may use the special rules the mission or scenario specifies for this type of Trooper.
*   The user of this Special Skill will be considered to be a Specialist Troop, even if he doesn't fulfill any of the defining characteristics of this type of Trooper (not being an [Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer"), [Doctor](/Doctor "Doctor"), [Hacker](/Hacker "Hacker"), etc.) in the scenario rules.
