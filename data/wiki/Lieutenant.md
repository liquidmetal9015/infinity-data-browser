The user of this Special Skill is the commanding officer of the combat force deployed by the player.

LIEUTENANT

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Private Information, Obligatory.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   The user of this Special Skill has a [Lieutenant Special Order](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order").
*   If, during the [Tactical Phase](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase") of the Turn, the Lieutenant is in [Isolated State](/Isolated_State "Isolated State") or any [Null](/Null "Null") State ([Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Dead](/Dead "Dead"), etc.) or has not been deployed on the gaming table, the [Loss of Lieutenant](/Loss_of_Lieutenant "Loss of Lieutenant") rule will be applied.
*   This Special Skill remains active even when its owner is in a [Null](/Null "Null") State, until a new Lieutenant is appointed. Once that happens, the owner of this Special Skill loses it.
*   When a new Lieutenant is appointed (due to Loss of Lieutenant, [Chain of Command](/Chain_of_Command "Chain of Command")...), they gain the Lieutenant Special Skill, with no additional abilities.
*   In the Unit Profile, this Special Skill may have additional abilities listed. Some examples are:
    *   **+1 Command Token**. The player that takes this Trooper as their Lieutenant will have one extra [Command Token](/Command_Tokens "Command Tokens") for the match.
    *   **+1 Order**. This indicates that this Lieutenant has two Special Lieutenant Orders each Turn instead of one.

  

IMPORTANT

*   The presence of a Trooper with the Lieutenant Special Skill in the player's [Army List](/Army_List "Army List") is obligatory.
*   The player's Army List cannot include more than one Trooper with this Special Skill.

Remotes ([REM](/REM "REM")) may not declare [Cautious Movement](/Cautious_Movement "Cautious Movement") or be chosen as Lieutenant.

[Irregular](/Irregular "Irregular") Troops may not be chosen as Lieutenant.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Inspiring Leadership](/Inspiring_Leadership "Inspiring Leadership")**  

*   In order to apply the effects of Inspiring Leadership, the user must be the Lieutenant of the Army, be on the game table, and not be in a [Null](/Null "Null") State ([Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Dead](/Dead "Dead"), [Sepsitorized](/Sepsitorized "Sepsitorized")...).

\[...\]

*   You may declare a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order") that includes the user by spending only the [Special Lieutenant Order](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order"), without expenditure of [Command Tokens](/Command_Token "Command Token") or extra Orders.

  
**[Mnemonica](/Mnemonica "Mnemonica")**  

*   The user may transfer his own [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Attribute value and the Mnemonica Special Skill to another Trooper in the same army. That Trooper must be on the battlefield in the form of a Model or Marker and have either a [Cube](/Cube "Cube") or the [REM](/REM "REM") Troop Type. If the user has the Lieutenant Special Skill this is also transferred, including any additional abilities and MODs such as (+1 Order).

  
**[NCO](/NCO "NCO")**  

*   Allows the user to replace any [Special Lieutenant Orders](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order") with [Tactical Orders](/Tactical_Order "Tactical Order") and to use them.

  
**[Strategos](/Strategos "Strategos")**  

*   In order to benefit from this Level of Strategos, the user must be the army's Lieutenant.

\[...\]

*   At the beginning of your Active Turn, during your [Order Count](/Order_Count "Order Count"), replace any [Special Lieutenant Orders](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order") with [Regular Orders](/Regular_Order "Regular Order"), adding them to the [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool") of the Lieutenant's [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group").
