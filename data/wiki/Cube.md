This indicates that the Trooper has an automated memory recording and storage device, commonly known as a Cube.

Troopers with a Cube are easier to heal. Additionally, Cube-wearing casualties can potentially be recovered for use in future missions.

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  
There are two different types of Cubes.

![cube.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/cube.png)

CUBE

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

EFFECTS

*   When using the [Doctor](/Doctor "Doctor") Special Skill (or other Special Skills or pieces of Equipment that explicitly say so) to heal a Trooper with a Cube, you can expend [Command Tokens](/Command_Token "Command Token") to re-roll a failed [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll.

  

![cube-2.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/cube-2.png)

CUBE 2.0

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

EFFECTS

*   When using the [Doctor](/Doctor "Doctor") Special Skill (or other Special Skills or pieces of Equipment that explicitly say so) to heal a Trooper with a Cube 2.0, you can expend [Command Tokens](/Command_Token "Command Token") to re-roll a failed [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll.
