ENGAGED STATE

ACTIVATION

*   The Trooper is in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with an [Enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") Trooper, and neither are in an [Immobilized](/Immobilized "Immobilized") or [Null](/Null "Null") State (except for [Sepsitorized](/Sepsitorized "Sepsitorized") or [Possessed](/Possessed "Possessed")).

EFFECTS

*   Troopers in Engaged State are considered to be in [Close Combat](/Close_Combat "Close Combat").
*   Troopers in this state can only declare **[Berserk](/Berserk "Berserk"), [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge"), [Idle](/Idle "Idle"), [Reset](/Reset "Reset")**, and Skills that specify that they can be used in Close Combat or in Engaged State.
*   Troopers that are in Engaged State may not trace [LoF](/LoF "LoF") outside their Close Combat.
*   By declaring [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") on a Trooper in Engaged State, the [BS Attack Into a Close Combat](/BS_Attack_Into_a_Close_Combat "BS Attack Into a Close Combat") rule must be applied.
*   [Template Weapons](/Template_Weapon "Template Weapon") placed on a group of Troopers in Engaged state will always affect **all participating Troopers**, even if the placement of the Template would only affect one of them.

CANCELLATION

*   This state is canceled when the Trooper is no longer in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with any [Enemy](/Enemy "Enemy") Troopers.
*   A Trooper's Engaged State is automatically canceled if, in the 5.1 [Effects step](/Effects_step "Effects step") of any Order, all enemies in Silhouette contact are in an [Immobilized](/Immobilized "Immobilized") or [Null](/Null "Null") State (except for [Sepsitorized](/Sepsitorized "Sepsitorized") or [Possessed](/Possessed "Possessed")).
*   A Trooper may also cancel the Engaged State if they pass a **[Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") Roll**, either [Normal](/Normal "Normal") or [Face to Face](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll"). In this case, the Trooper must leave Silhouette contact, moving up to 2 inches to separate themselves from the enemy.
*   If they cannot move to a valid position that is outside Silhouette contact, the Trooper will remain in Engaged State without moving.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Line of Fire](/Line_of_Fire "Line of Fire")**

*   Troopers who are in Engaged State **cannot** draw LoF to Troopers or game elements that are not in Engaged State with them. In addition, Troopers who are in Engaged State are not affected by [Visibility Conditions](/Visibility_Conditions "Visibility Conditions") or by other rules affecting LoF.

  
**[Guts Roll](/Guts_Roll "Guts Roll") Requirements**

*   The Trooper is not in an Engaged, [Null](/Null "Null") or [IMM](/IMM "IMM") State.
