If a target receives **as many [Wounds](/Wounds "Wounds")** as the value of its [VITA](/VITA "VITA")/[STR](/STR "STR") Attribute, it enters [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State").

On the other hand, if the target receives **more Wounds** than the value of its VITA/STR Attribute, it enters Dead State.

DEAD

[Null](/Labels "Labels")

ACTIVATION

*   A Trooper or a game element ("Trooper" from now on) receives more [Wounds](/Wounds "Wounds") than their [VITA](/VITA "VITA")/[STR](/STR "STR") Attribute value.
*   A Trooper in the [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State") receives more Wounds.

EFFECTS

*   The Trooper is removed from the game table and their [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group").
*   The Trooper ceases to generate [Orders](/Orders "Orders") in successive [Player Turns](/Player_Turn "Player Turn").
*   The Trooper does not count towards the Player's [Victory Points](/Victory_Points "Victory Points").

CANCELLATION

*   Dead State is irreversible and cannot be cancelled, unless the rules specific to the mission or scenario being played indicate otherwise. In that case, follow the procedure stated in the rules.
