Users of this Special Skill are exceptional sharpshooters, adept at hitting their target's weakest spots and making every shot count.

MARKSMANSHIP

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user may only benefit from this Special Skill when he declares a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack").

EFFECTS

*   The user may ignore the negative [BS](/BS "BS") MODs imposed by [Partial Cover](/Partial_Cover "Partial Cover"), and [Nanoscreen](/Nanoscreen "Nanoscreen").

  

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

IMPORTANT

Troopers with the [Multispectral Visor](/Multispectral_Visor "Multispectral Visor") piece of Equipment or with the Marksmanship Special Skill are affected by:

*   [White Noise Zones](/White_Noise_Zone "White Noise Zone").
*   The [Reflective](/Reflective "Reflective") Trait.
*   The [Albedo](/Albedo "Albedo") piece of Equipment.
