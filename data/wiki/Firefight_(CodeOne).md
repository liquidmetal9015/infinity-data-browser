Mission Objectives
------------------

*   To have **more** surviving Specialist Troops than the adversary at the end of the game (2 Objective Points).
*   To Kill **more** Specialist Troops than the adversary (1 Objective Point).
*   To Kill **more** [Lieutenants](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") than the adversary (3 Objective Points).
*   To Kill **more** [Army Points](/Army_Points "Army Points") than the adversary (4 Objective Points).

Scenario Special Rules
----------------------

### Killing

Troopers are considered Killed by the adversary when they enter [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State"), or they are in a [Null](/Null "Null") State at the end of the game.

Troopers that **have not been deployed on the game table**, as a [Model](/Model "Model") or [Marker](/Marker "Marker"), at the end of the game will be considered to be Killed by the adversary.

### Reinforced Tactical Link

In this mission, the identity of the [Lieutenant](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") is always [Open Information](/Open_Information "Open Information"). The player must indicate which Marker is the Lieutenant if it is in a [Marker](/Marker "Marker") state.

The Lieutenant must be placed on the game table at the beginning of the first [Game Round](/Game_Round "Game Round"), either as a model or as a Marker.

If players lack a Lieutenant at the start of the [Tactical Phase](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase") of their Active Turn, because this Trooper was not deployed or because it is in a [Null](/Null "Null") state ([Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Dead](/Dead "Dead")…), then they must name a new Lieutenant, without Order expenditure. The identity of this new Lieutenant is also Open Information. It is compulsory such Lieutenant be a Model or a Marker placed on the game table.

### Designated Landing Area

The whole game table is considered a Designated Landing Area. Troopers possessing the [Combat Jump](/Combat_Jump "Combat Jump") Special Skill can apply a +3 MOD to their deployment [PH](/PH "PH") Roll. This MOD is cumulative with any other MOD provided by any other rule.

Additionally, Troopers who have a Special Skill with the [Airborne Deployment](/Airborne_Deployment "Airborne Deployment") Label (AD) will ignore the prohibition of deploying inside the enemy [Deployment Zone](/Deployment_Zone "Deployment Zone").

### Specialist Troops

For the purposes of this scenario, only [Doctors](/Doctor "Doctor"), [Engineers](/Engineer "Engineer"), [Forward Observers](/Forward_Observer "Forward Observer"), [Hackers](/Hacker "Hacker"), [Paramedics](/Paramedic "Paramedic"), [Specialist Operatives](/Specialist_Operative "Specialist Operative"), and Troopers possessing the [Chain of Command](/Chain_of_Command "Chain of Command") Special Skill are considered Specialist Troops.

Doctors and Engineers cannot make use of [Peripherals](/Peripheral "Peripheral") to perform tasks reserved for Specialist Troops.

  

End of the Mission
------------------

This scenario has a limited time frame, so it will automatically finish at the end of the **third [Game Round](/Game_Round "Game Round")**.

If a player finds, during the [Tactical Phase](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase") of his [Player Turn](/Player_Turn "Player Turn"), that all Troopers in their [Army List](/Army_List "Army List") are in a [Null](/Null "Null") state, the mission will finish at the end of this Player Turn.
