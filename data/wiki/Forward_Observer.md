This Special Skill characterizes Troopers who move before the bulk of their own forces and take positions beyond the established allied lines.

FORWARD OBSERVER

SHORT SKILL / ARO

[BS Attack, Optional](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user must be able to draw [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to the target.

EFFECTS

*   The use of Forward Observer is a form of Attack with a [BS Weapon](/BS_Weapon "BS Weapon") with the [BS Weapon (WIP)](/BS_Weapon_\(WIP\) "BS Weapon (WIP)") Trait, and instead of forcing the target to make a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll"), causes them to enter [Targeted State](/Targeted_State "Targeted State").
*   Place a Targeted Token (TARGETED) beside the target of a successful Forward Observer attack.
