This is a device that generates a thick cloud of nanobots that protects its user against ranged Attacks.

NANOSCREEN

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user is the target of a **[BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack")**.

EFFECTS

*   A -3 BS MOD is imposed on BS Attack Rolls against the user, and a +3 MOD applies to the user's [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") against BS Attacks.
*   **Restriction:** These MODs do not apply to BS Attacks with the [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack") Trait.

  

REMEMBER

This device cannot be used against **[CC Attacks](/CC_Attack "CC Attack")**.
