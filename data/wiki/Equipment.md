Same as Skills, pieces of Equipment allow a Trooper to perform unique actions or gain exclusive benefits. These special Effects provided by Equipment are detailed next.

IMPORTANT

Some pieces of Equipment can have different Levels, and these are not cumulative. Therefore, Troopers may only use the MODs and special rules of a piece of Equipment specific to the Level they have.
