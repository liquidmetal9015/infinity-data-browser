DIFFICULT TERRAIN

Some of the battlefield’s zones may feature certain advantages or impairments to the Movement of Troopers attempting to cross them.

REQUIREMENTS

*   Troopers must apply the Difficult Terrain rules when their [Silhouette](/Silhouette "Silhouette") is inside or in contact with a Difficult Terrain area and they declare a Skill with the [Movement Label](/Movement_Label "Movement Label") such as [Move](/Move "Move"), [Cautious Movement](/Cautious_Movement "Cautious Movement"), [Climb](/Climb "Climb"), [Jump](/Jump "Jump"), or the [Berserk](/Berserk "Berserk") Special Skill.
*   Difficult Terrain also affects Troopers who make [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with it during an Order.

EFFECTS

*   Entering Silhouette contact with a Difficult Terrain area immediately and automatically ends the Trooper's Movement, regardless of its type, for the rest of the [Order](/Trooper_Activation "Trooper Activation").
*   If the Trooper chooses to continue to move through the Difficult Terrain area, they must declare a new [Order](/Order "Order") and apply a MOD of -1 inch to both values of their [MOV](/MOV "MOV") Attribute, or to the value specified by any Skill involved, such as [Jump](/Jump "Jump").
