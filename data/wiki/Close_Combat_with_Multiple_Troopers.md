Close Combat (CC)
-----------------

Urban combat, attempting to take a building, or ship to ship space-boarding often occur in reduced spaces, which favors close combat with bladed weapons (knives, machetes, sabers, swords, claws, etc.) which we generically call Melee Weapons.

  

Interacting with a Close Combat
-------------------------------

Any Trooper in [Silhouette](/Silhouette "Silhouette") contact with an [Allied](/Allied "Allied") Model that is in the [Engaged State](/Engaged_State "Engaged State") may only declare [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") or other related Skills that may be used in CC. For example, in these circumstances declaring Skills such as [Doctor](/Doctor "Doctor") or [Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer") on a Trooper in Engaged State is not allowed.

SEE ALSO

**Ballistic Skills > [BS Attack Into a Close Combat](/BS_Attack_Into_a_Close_Combat "BS Attack Into a Close Combat")**  
If a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") is declared against an enemy Trooper that is engaged in CC Combat a -6 MOD must be applied for each Allied Trooper engaged in the CC (in addition to any MODs for [Range](/Range "Range"), [Cover](/Cover "Cover"), [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism")... for the target).

Every failed BS Attack Roll will hit the Allied Trooper, forcing them to perform a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll"). If there are several Allied Troopers locked in that Close Combat, then the Trooper’s player chooses which one of them receives each hit.

  

Movement Sequence and CC
------------------------

Movement Sequence and CC

![eng-cc-movement.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/eng-cc-movement.jpg)

Close Combat is often associated with a Movement, the most common game sequence in this case is the following:

**Step 1:**  
The Active Player (Trooper A) consumes an [Order](/Order "Order") and declares [Move](/Move "Move").

**Step 2:**  
The Reactive Player (Trooper B) declares [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") as their [ARO](/ARO "ARO").

**Step 3:**  
The Active Player (Trooper A) declares CC Attack as the second Short Skill of the Order.

**Step 4:**  
The corresponding [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") is performed. Face to Face Roll:

*   Active Player: 1 Die in CC.
*   Reactive Player: 1 Die in CC.

  

CC Attack Modifiers (MOD)
-------------------------

Before performing the [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") Roll (or Rolls), the player must check every MOD they must apply. The MODs are:

*   Close Combat against several enemies.
*   Special Skills, Weapons and Equipment.

  

### Close Combat With Multiple Troopers

This rule is applied when more than 2 Troopers are in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the target and any of them is activated.

#### During the Active Turn

When declaring a [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), the Player must select **one** [Trooper](/Trooper "Trooper") or [Peripheral](/Peripheral "Peripheral") activated by the Order. **That Trooper or Peripheral performs** the [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") and has a +1 MOD to [Burst](/Burst "Burst") (B) for each [Allied](/Allied "Allied") Trooper or Peripheral that is **not** in a [Null](/Null "Null") or [Immobilized](/Immobilized "Immobilized") State and is in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with their target.

#### During the Reactive Turn

When declaring [AROs](/ARO "ARO"), if the Reactive player decides to declare [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") they must select **only one** Trooper or Peripheral in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the target. The selected Trooper performs a CC Attack and has a +1 MOD to [B](/B "B") **for each [Allied](/Allied "Allied") Trooper or Peripheral** in Silhouette contact with the target. The MOD is only applied for those allies that are not in a [Null](/Null "Null") or [Immobilized](/Immobilized "Immobilized") State, and have not declared [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge"), [Idle](/Idle "Idle") or [Reset](/Reset "Reset").

  

Other CC Restrictions
---------------------

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

*   When in Close Combat, Troopers can only declare:
    *   [Berserk](/Berserk "Berserk")
    *   [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack")
    *   [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge")
    *   [Idle](/Idle "Idle")
    *   [Reset](/Reset "Reset")
    *   Those Skills that specify that they can be used in Close Combat or in [Engaged State](/Engaged_State "Engaged State").
*   The maximum number of Models that may be in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with a Model on a 25 mm base is 4.
*   The maximum number of Models that may be in Silhouette contact with a Model on a 40 mm or larger base is 6.

**Original**  

*   When in Close Combat it is only possible to declare [Berserk](/Berserk "Berserk"), [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge"), [Idle](/Idle "Idle"), [Reset](/Reset "Reset"), and Skills that specify that they can be used in Close Combat or in [Engaged State](/Engaged_State "Engaged State").
*   The maximum number of Models that may be in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with a Model on a 25 mm base is 4.
*   The maximum number of Models that may be in Silhouette contact with a Model on a 40 mm or larger base is 6.

  

Examples
--------

Close Combat Against Several Enemies, Active Turn

![eng-cc-example-1.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/eng-cc-example-1.jpg)

![eng-cc-example-2.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/eng-cc-example-2.jpg)

In the image we have a Fennec and a Lambda [Doctor](/Doctor "Doctor") with his [Peripheral](/Peripheral "Peripheral") (Servant) Yudbot.

**Step 1:**  
The Active Trooper (the Lambda) consumes an [Order](/Order "Order") and declares [Move](/Move "Move").

**Step 2:**  
The Reactive Trooper (the Fennec) declares a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") against the Doctor as their [ARO](/ARO "ARO").

**Step 3:**  
The Active Trooper declares [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"). The player chooses the Doctor to perform the CC Attack.

The Doctor obtains a +1[B](/B "B") MOD for having his Peripheral (Servant) in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with his target.

**Step 4:**  
Measurements for the BS Attack are made and the dice are rolled.

Face to Face Roll:

*   Active Player: CC Attack with 2 Dice.
*   Reactive Player: BS Attack with 1 Die.

  

Close Combat Against Several Enemies, Reactive Turn

In the Blue player's Active Turn, a [Move](/Move "Move") + [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") Order is added to the previous example's melee:

**Step 1:**  
The Active Trooper (the Fennec) consumes an [Order](/Order "Order") and declares [Move](/Move "Move") to reach [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the [Peripheral](/Peripheral "Peripheral") (Servant).

![eng-cc-example-3.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/eng-cc-example-3.jpg)

**Step 2:**  
Since the Troopers involved in a Close Combat do not have [LoF](/LoF "LoF") outside Close Combat, they cannot declare [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack"). Therefore, the Reactive Player's Troopers (Purple) can only declare [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), [Reset](/Reset "Reset"), [Idle](/Idle "Idle"), or [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") with their Lambda or Peripheral (Servant).

They decide to declare [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") as an [ARO](/ARO "ARO") with their Peripheral (Servant).

**Step 3:**  
As the second half of the Order, the Active Trooper declares [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack").

The Fennec gets a +1[B](/B "B") MOD thanks to his partner being in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the [Peripheral](/Peripheral "Peripheral") (Servant).

The Peripheral does not get a +1B MOD because its Controller is not in Silhouette contact with the Active Trooper.

![eng-cc-example-4.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/eng-cc-example-4.jpg)

**Step 4:**  
The corresponding Rolls are performed.

Face to Face Roll:

*   Active Player. CC Attack with 2 Dice.
*   Reactive Player: CC Attack with 1 Die.

Special Skills and Equipment
----------------------------
