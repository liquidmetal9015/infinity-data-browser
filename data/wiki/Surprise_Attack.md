The user of this Special Skill is able to catch their adversaries unaware by declaring Attacks that are difficult to avoid.

SURPRISE ATTACK

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   To use this Special Skill, Troopers must be **in Marker form** (for example, [Camouflaged](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged"), [Impersonation](/Impersonation_State "Impersonation State"), [Decoy](/Decoy_State "Decoy State"), [Holoecho](/Holoecho "Holoecho")...) or in [Hidden Deployment](/Hidden_Deployment_State "Hidden Deployment State") at the start of the Order in which they are activated.
*   The user must declare a [CC Attack](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack"), or [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack").
*   The user of this Special Skill must be in their [Active Turn](/Active_Turn "Active Turn").

EFFECTS

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

*   This Attack imposes an additional negative MOD, as shown in round brackets in the [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile") \[Surprise Attack (-3), Surprise Attack (-6)...\]. This MOD **only applies to targets of the Attack** and applies to any Face to Face Roll that those targets perform in [ARO](/ARO "ARO").
*   If a specific Attribute is listed between brackets in the Unit Profile, such as Surprise Attack (CC-6), the negative MOD **only applies to that Attribute** of the targets of the Attack. This MOD is **not** cumulative with other MODs imposed by Surprise Attack, for example if the Trooper has both Surprise Attack (-3) and Surprise Attack (CC-6).
*   **Restriction:** This Skill cannot be used again until the Trooper returns to Marker form.

**Original**  

*   This Attack imposes an additional negative MOD, as shown in round brackets in the [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile") \[Surprise Attack (-3), Surprise Attack (-6)...\]. This MOD **only applies to targets of the Attack** and applies to any Skill Roll that those targets perform in [ARO](/ARO "ARO").
*   If a specific Attribute is listed between brackets in the Unit Profile, such as Surprise Attack (CC-6), the negative MOD **only applies to that Attribute** of the targets of the Attack. This MOD is **not** cumulative with other MODs imposed by Surprise Attack, for example if the Trooper has both Surprise Attack (-3) and Surprise Attack (CC-6).
*   **Restriction:** This Skill cannot be used again until the Trooper returns to Marker form.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Biometric Visor](/Biometric_Visor "Biometric Visor")**

*   Users of a Biometric Visor ignore Surprise Attack MODs from attackers benefitting from [Impersonation](/Impersonation_State "Impersonation State") or [Holoecho States](/Holoecho_State "Holoecho State"), if they have [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to the attacker. In addition, they also ignore Surprise Attack MODs from [CC Attacks](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") from those attackers even if they don't have LoF to the attacker.

**[Combat Instinct](/Combat_Instinct "Combat Instinct")**

*   The user ignores **Surprise Attack** MODs from attackers.

**[Multispectral Visor](/Multispectral_Visor "Multispectral Visor")**

*   Users of a Multispectral Visor L3 ignore Surprise Attack MODs if they have [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to the attacker. In addition, they also ignore Surprise Attack MODs from [CC Attacks](/CC_Attack "CC Attack") even if they don't have LoF to the attacker.

N5 FAQ

Version: 0.0.0, Oct 2025

**Q: If multiple Troopers use Surprise Attack against the same target, for example via a Coordinated Order, or a Controller and Peripheral, do the Surprise Attack MODs stack with each other?**

A: No. Each Trooper's Surprise Attack MOD will be applied separately.
