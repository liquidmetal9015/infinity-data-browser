This Special Skill ties the behavior of entire armies to the user's discipline and state of mind.

**Update** _PDF 5.2, October 2025_  

INSPIRING LEADERSHIP

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   If the user belongs to a [Fireteam](/Fireteam "Fireteam") where all members have the [Regular](/Regular "Regular") Training Characteristic listed in their **[Unit Profiles](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile")**, apply a +2 inch bonus to the Team Leader’s [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") when making [Coherency](/Coherency "Coherency") Checks for the Fireteam.
*   **Restriction:** This bonus is not cumulative with any bonus to the Team Leader’s Zone of Control.
*   If the user is the **[Lieutenant](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant")** of the Army, is on the game table, and not in a [Null](/Null "Null") State ([Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Dead](/Dead "Dead"), [Sepsitorized](/Sepsitorized "Sepsitorized")…), the following effects are applied:
    *   All Troopers in the [Army List](/Army_List "Army List") that provide Orders:
        *   Possess the [Regular](/Regular "Regular") Training Characteristic.
        *   Have the [Courage](/Courage "Courage") Special Skill. Troopers with the [Religious Troop](/Religious_Troop "Religious Troop") Special Skill don’t apply this Effect.
    *   You may declare a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order") that includes the user by spending only the [Special Lieutenant Order](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order"), without expenditure of [Command Tokens](/Command_Token "Command Token") or extra Orders.
    *   The army is not affected by the Retreat! rule, and never enters a [Retreat! situation](/Retreat_Situation "Retreat Situation").
*   If the user **becomes the [Lieutenant](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant")** of the Army (for example via the [Chain of Command](/Chain_of_Command "Chain of Command") Special Skill or after a [Loss of Lieutenant](/Loss_of_Lieutenant "Loss of Lieutenant") situation), the previous Effects of Inspiring Leadership apply immediately after the user has been appointed as Lieutenant.

INSPIRING LEADERSHIP

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   In order to apply the effects of Inspiring Leadership, the user must be the [Lieutenant](/Lieutenant "Lieutenant") of the Army, be on the game table, and not be in a [Null](/Null "Null") State ([Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Dead](/Dead "Dead"), [Sepsitorized](/Sepsitorized "Sepsitorized")...).

EFFECTS

*   All Troopers in the [Army List](/Army_List "Army List") that provide Orders:
    *   Possess the [Regular](/Regular "Regular") Training Characteristic.
    *   Have the [Courage](/Courage "Courage") Special Skill. Troopers with the [Religious Troop](/Religious_Troop "Religious Troop") Special Skill don't apply this Effect.
*   You may declare a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order") that includes the user by spending only the [Special Lieutenant Order](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order"), without expenditure of [Command Tokens](/Command_Token "Command Token") or extra Orders.
*   If the user belongs to a [Fireteam](/Fireteam "Fireteam") where all members already have the Regular Training Characteristic in their **[Unit Profiles](/Unit_Profiles "Unit Profiles")**, apply a +2 inch bonus to the Team Leader's [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") when making [Coherency](/Coherency "Coherency") Checks for the Fireteam.
*   The army is not affected by the Retreat! rule, and never enters a [Retreat! situation](/Retreat_Situation "Retreat Situation").
