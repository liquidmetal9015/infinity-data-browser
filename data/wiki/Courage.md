COURAGE

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   Courage allows its user to choose to automatically pass any [Guts Rolls](/Guts_Rolls "Guts Rolls").
*   Troopers with the Courage Special Skill are unaffected by [Retreat! situations](/Retreat_Situation "Retreat Situation"), so they cannot enter [Retreat! State](/Retreat_State "Retreat State") and act normally until the end of the game.
*   However, even in a Retreat! situation, Troops possessing Courage are affected by [Loss of Lieutenant](/Loss_of_Lieutenant "Loss of Lieutenant").
