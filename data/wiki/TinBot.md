A TinBot is a semi-autonomous piece of Equipment that remains near its owner and provides him with some bonus or benefit.

![tinbot0.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/tinbot0.jpg)

TINBOT

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Comms Equipment, Prior Deployment.](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   A TinBot grants its MODs or additional advantages as long as its owner is not in any [Null](/Null "Null") State ([Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Dead](/Dead "Dead"), [Sepsitorized](/Sepsitorized "Sepsitorized"), etc.) or [Isolated](/Isolated "Isolated") State.

EFFECTS

*   TinBot Models and Tokens are State Tokens, so they cannot block movement or [LoF](/LoF "LoF"), or be targeted by any Skill.
*   If the owner of a TinBot enters [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State"), remove the TinBot from the game alongside its owner.
*   A TinBot grants its owner with different MODs, Special Skills, or pieces of Equipment. The player will apply the one specified next to the TinBot, in the [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile").
*   If the owner of a TinBot is part of a [Fireteam](/Fireteam "Fireteam"), every member of the Fireteam benefits from the TinBot.
*   If the members of a Fireteam have more than one TinBot between them that grants the same advantages, they can only use one of them per declared [Order](/Trooper_Activation "Trooper Activation") or [ARO](/ARO "ARO"). If the TinBots grant different MODs for the same advantage, only the most advantageous MOD may be used.

  

Examples of Tinbot Types

**TinBot: [Firewall](/Firewall "Firewall") (-3)**  
Indicates that, when the owner of the TinBot is targeted by a [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack"), the Enemy applies a -3 Firewall MOD.

**TinBot: [Guided](/Guided "Guided") (-6)**  
Indicates that, when the owner of the TinBot is targeted by a Guided Attack, the Enemy applies a -6 MOD.

**TinBot: [Discover](/Discover "Discover") (+3)**  
Indicates that, when the owner of the TinBot declares the Discover Skill, they apply a +3 WIP MOD.
