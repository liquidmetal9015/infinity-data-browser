IMMOBILIZED-B

ACTIVATION

*   The Trooper or game element ("Trooper" from now on) suffers a successful [Attack](/Attack "Attack") or Effect using an [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition"), [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") or a game condition or Scenario Special Rule, capable of causing this state.

EFFECTS

*   Troopers in Immobilized-B State cannot declare any Skill, [Attack](/Attack "Attack") or [ARO](/ARO "ARO"), except [Reset](/Reset "Reset") applying a -3 [WIP](/WIP "WIP") MOD.
*   Automatic Special Skills and Equipment continue to work, but the Trooper must still respect all declaration restrictions.
*   Troopers in the Immobilized-B state continue to provide [Orders](/Orders "Orders") for their [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool").

CANCELLATION

*   The affected Trooper may cancel this state via a successful [Normal](/Normal "Normal") or [Face to Face](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") [Reset](/Reset "Reset") Roll, applying the -3 [WIP](/WIP "WIP") MOD provided by this state.
*   A Trooper with the **[Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer")** Special Skill (or an equivalent Skill) may cancel the Immobilized-B state by spending one [Short Skill](/Short_Skill "Short Skill") of an [Order](/Trooper_Activation "Trooper Activation") while in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the affected Trooper, and passing a [Normal](/Normal "Normal") [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll (or the Roll specified by the Special Skill or Scenario).

  

SEE ALSO

**[Carbonite](/Carbonite "Carbonite")**

*   Any failed Saving Roll causes the target to enter **Immobilized-B State**, placing an Immobilized-B State Token (IMM-B) by them.

  
**[Electromagnetic (E/M) Ammunition](/Electromagnetic_\(E/M\)_Ammunition "Electromagnetic (E/M) Ammunition")**

*   If the target fails a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") and is Heavy Infantry ([HI](/HI "HI")), a [TAG](/TAG "TAG"), a Remote ([REM](/REM "REM")), or a Vehicle ([VH](/VH "VH")), then it also enters Immobilized-B State. Place an Immobilized-B Token (IMM-B) next to it in addition to the [Isolated](/Isolated "Isolated") Token.

  
**[Cybermines](/Mines#Cybermines "Mines")**

*   However, any Troopers with the **[Hackable](/Hackable "Hackable") Characteristic ([HI](/HI "HI"), [REM](/REM "REM"), [TAG](/TAG "TAG"), [VH](/VH "VH")...)** and **[Hackers](/Hackers "Hackers")** who fail either [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") enter **Immobilized-B State** instead of [Stunned State](/Stunned_State "Stunned State").

  
**[Guts Roll](/Guts_Roll "Guts Roll") Requirements**

*   The Trooper is not in an [Engaged](/Engaged_State "Engaged State"), [Null](/Null "Null") or [IMM](/IMM "IMM") State.
