POSSESSED STATE

[Null](/Labels "Labels")

ACTIVATION

*   The Trooper or game element ("Trooper" from now on) suffers a successful [Attack](/Attack "Attack") or Effect using an [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition"), [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") or a game condition or Scenario Special Rule, capable of causing this state.

EFFECTS

*   **Restriction:** Troopers in this state **cannot be activated or receive [Orders](/Orders "Orders") from their player's [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool").** While they are Possessed, Troopers are considered to be [enemies](/Enemy "Enemy") by the rest of their owner's Troopers and are considered to be [allies](/Allied "Allied") by the Troopers of the player that caused this game state.
*   Troopers in this state do **not** contribute [Orders](/Orders "Orders") during the [Tactical Phases](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase") of either player.
*   Troopers in this state may be activated and receive Orders from the [Order Pool](/Order_Pool "Order Pool") of the [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group") of the Trooper who caused them to enter the Possessed state. A Possessed Trooper does not count towards the maximum number of members the Combat Group may contain.
*   Possessed Troopers must use the Possessed Trooper profile instead of their own.

Note\* Use the Trooper's original [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute") values.

*   This state does not interfere with Automatic Special Skills or Automatic Equipment, which will remain functioning normally.
*   A Possessed Trooper does **not** count towards either Players' [Victory Points](/Victory_Points "Victory Points").
*   **Restriction:** A Trooper in Possessed State cannot be part of a [Fireteam](/Fireteam "Fireteam"). If a member of a Fireteam enters Possessed State they automatically stop being part of the Fireteam.

CANCELLATION

*   In the owning player's [Tactical Phase](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase"), during the [Executive Use of Command Tokens](/Executive_Use_of_Command_Tokens "Executive Use of Command Tokens") step, the player can automatically cancel this state by spending 1 [Command Token](/Command_Token "Command Token").
*   The Possessed State is automatically canceled if the owning player successfully affects the Possessed Trooper with the [Total Control](/Total_Control "Total Control") Hacking Program.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Total Control](/Total_Control "Total Control")**

*   Any failed [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") causes the target to enter Possessed State, placing a Possessed State Token (POS) by them. If this Program is used against a [TAG](/TAG "TAG") in Possessed State, any failed Saving Roll cancels the target's Possessed State, causing them to enter [Normal State](/Normal_State "Normal State"), and the Possessed State Token (POS) is removed.
