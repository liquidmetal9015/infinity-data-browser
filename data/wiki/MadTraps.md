Within military confines, the term tactical perimeter devices is used for automated zone defense systems.

Some examples of Perimeter Weapons are CrazyKoalas, MadTraps, [WildParrots](/WildParrot "WildParrot"), and all other Weapons or pieces of Equipment with the [Perimeter](/Perimeter "Perimeter") Trait.

PERIMETER WEAPONS

EFFECTS

*   These weapons are placed on the game table using the [Place Deployable](/Place_Deployable "Place Deployable") Common Skill, applying the Perimeter and Deployable rule.
*   Therefore, when players declare the Place Deployable Common Skill, they place the Perimeter Weapon or piece of Equipment totally inside the [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control") of the Trooper, instead of placing it in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact").

  

Perimeter Weapons and the Boost Trait
-------------------------------------

These devices have been specifically designed to protect the immediate perimeter of their user, and also to detect and/or dissuade intrusion attempts during the user's security duties.

In the [Glossary](/Glossary "Glossary") you can consult all the [Traits](/Traits "Traits") that indicate how a weapon operates.

BOOST

EFFECTS

*   Once the weapon with the Boost Trait is in play on the game table, it is triggered when an enemy Model declares or performs an [Order](/Order "Order") or [ARO](/ARO "ARO") in its [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control"). When triggered, the weapon must move until it enters [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the enemy Model, and then detonate. The only way to avoid this attack is a successful [Normal](/Normal "Normal") [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") Roll. Once the weapon has detonated, it is removed from the game.
*   The weapon will not trigger if the path from the weapon to the enemy Model is blocked, for example by an impassable obstacle (for example a wall of infinite height, a closed door or sealed room...) or a gap too small for the Silhouette Template of the weapon to pass through.
*   The weapon is not triggered by [Camouflage Markers](/Camouflaged "Camouflaged") (CAMO), [Impersonation](/Impersonation "Impersonation") Markers (IMP-1 and IMP-2), or by those Markers who specify so in their description.
*   Due to the [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Trait, the weapon does not activate other [Deployable Weapons](/Deployable_Weapon "Deployable Weapon") or Equipment.

N5 FAQ

Version: 0.0.0, Oct 2025

**Q: Do Troopers with Aerial Special Skill activate Weapons with the Boost Trait?**

A: No, as it is not possible to enter [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with Aerial Troopers.
