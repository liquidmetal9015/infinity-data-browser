A type of ammunition devised to incapacitate its target without killing it.

STUN AMMUNITION

ROLL

*   After a successful attack with Stun Ammunition, the target must make one [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") per hit suffered.

EFFECTS

*   A failed Saving Roll against Stun Ammunition causes the target to enter [Stunned State](/Stunned_State "Stunned State"). Place a Stunned State Token (STUNNED) next to it.
*   Additionally, failing the Saving Roll causes the target to automatically fail the subsequent [Guts Roll](/Guts_Roll "Guts Roll") for having survived an Attack, unless he has the [Courage](/Courage "Courage") Special Skill or an equivalent.
*   [Critical](/Critical "Critical") hits with Stun Ammunition cause the target to make an additional Saving Roll.
