The Dazer is Deployable Equipment that creates a Terrain Zone in its Zone of Control, which hinders movement for other Troopers.

![dazer0.jpg](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/dazer0.jpg)

DAZER

AUTOMATIC EQUIPMENT

[Deployable, Disposable (3), Zone of Control.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   This piece of Equipment generates a [Difficult Terrain](/Difficult_Terrain "Difficult Terrain") area of infinite height in its [Zone of Control](/Zone_of_Control "Zone of Control").
*   Troopers with the [Terrain](/Terrain "Terrain") (Total) Special Skill ignore the effects of the Dazer and benefit from their MOD when declaring Skills with the [Movement Label](/Movement_Label "Movement Label").
*   A deployed Dazer remains on the table until the end of the game, or until it is destroyed.
