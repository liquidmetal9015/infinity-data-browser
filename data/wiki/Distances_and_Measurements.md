Distances on the game table are measured in inches, using a tape measure or ruler.

When measuring the distance between two Troopers, players must measure between the **closest parts** of their [Silhouettes](/Silhouette "Silhouette").

To determine what the distance is between two objects or scenery items, players must measure a straight line between them.

  

![sibylla-n5.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/sibylla-n5.png)

Sibylla's Advice

In Infinity, it is advised to use the base for measuring distances. By doing so, it is easier to determine the reference point for the distance and speed up the game. Only on rare occasions it will be necessary to use the [Silhouette Template](/Silhouette_Template "Silhouette Template") to know the exact range between two Troopers.

  

REMEMBER

As a general rule in Infinity, the Skills that compose an [Order](/Trooper_Activation "Trooper Activation") must be declared before taking any measurements (see [Order Expenditure Sequence](/Order_Expenditure_Sequence "Order Expenditure Sequence")).
