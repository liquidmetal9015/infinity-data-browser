Fireteam Creation
-----------------

Each Army has a Fireteams Chart that indicates the quantity and type of Fireteams that the player may create during the game.

*   **During the [Deployment Phase](/Deployment_Phase "Deployment Phase")**, place a Fireteam Leader Token (FIRETEAM LEADER), or equivalent, beside one of the Troopers that meet the Requirements of this rule. In that moment, the player must make a [Coherency](/Coherency "Coherency") Check relative to the chosen Fireteam Leader.
*   **During the game**, spend a [Command Token](/Command_Token "Command Token") and place a Fireteam Leader Token (FIRETEAM LEADER) beside one of the Troopers that meet the instructions of the [Fireteams Chart](/Fireteams_Chart "Fireteams Chart") of their Army.

  

SEE ALSO

Creating a Fireteam during the game requires the **[Operational Use of a Command Token](/Command_Tokens#Command_Tokens:_Operational_Use "Command Tokens")**.

  

IMPORTANT

When a Fireteam is created, the player must declare which Troopers are part of it, and must follow all the rules and instructions in the [Fireteams Chart](/Fireteams_Chart "Fireteams Chart").

  

IMPORTANT

The following Troopers cannot be part of a Fireteam:

*   [Peripherals](/Peripheral "Peripheral") and their Controllers.
*   Troopers in [Marker](/Marker "Marker") form.
*   Troopers with the [Infiltration](/Infiltration "Infiltration") Special Skill.
*   Troopers possessing Skills with the [Airborne Deployment](/Airborne_Deployment "Airborne Deployment") Label.
*   Troopers that activate or are in the [Suppressive Fire State](/Suppressive_Fire_State "Suppressive Fire State").
*   Troopers who deploy in [Decoy State](/Decoy_State "Decoy State").
*   Troopers in [Isolated State](/Isolated_State "Isolated State") or any [Null](/Null "Null") State.

Additionally, members of a Fireteam cannot be part of a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order").

  

Rejoining the Fireteam in the States Phase
------------------------------------------

At the end of the next [States Phase](/States_Phase "States Phase"), any Troopers who left a Fireteam may automatically rejoin it if they are in [Coherency](/Coherency "Coherency") with the Fireteam Leader.

  

Fireteam Types
--------------

The types of Fireteams that the player may use during a match are:

### Fireteam Duo

![n4-fireteam-type-duo-450.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n4-fireteam-type-duo-450.png) When created, a Fireteam Duo **must** contain two Troopers.

### Fireteam Haris

![n4-fireteam-type-haris-450.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n4-fireteam-type-haris-450.png) When created, a Fireteam Haris **must** contain three Troopers.

### Fireteam Core

![n4-fireteam-type-core-450.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n4-fireteam-type-core-450.png) When created, a Fireteam Core **must** contain a minimum of three and a maximum of five Troopers.

  

IMPORTANT

Some [Sectorials](/Army_List#Sectorial_Army_Lists "Army List") can modify the conditions for creating a Fireteam, which will be listed in the Sectorial’s [Fireteams Chart](/Fireteams_Chart "Fireteams Chart").

  

General Rules for Fireteams
---------------------------

*   The members of a Fireteam must observe the **[Coherency](/Coherency "Coherency")** rule in regard to the **Fireteam Leader**.
*   A Trooper **cannot** simultaneously be part of more than one Fireteam.
*   All members of a Fireteam must belong to the same [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group").
*   When a [Regular Order](/Regular_Order "Regular Order") is spent on a member of a Fireteam, they automatically become the Fireteam Leader. Additionally, they activate the other members of the Fireteam (see [Fireteam Order](/Fireteam_Order "Fireteam Order")).
*   Fireteams must contain a minimum of two Troopers and a maximum of five Troopers (always observing the Army's [Fireteams Chart](/Fireteams_Chart "Fireteams Chart")).
*   Fireteams allow the use of the **[Fireteam Bonuses](/Fireteam_Bonuses "Fireteam Bonuses")** for two, three, four, or five members, depending on the [Fireteam Level](/Fireteam_Bonuses#Fireteam_Level_Chart "Fireteam Bonuses").

  

Fireteams and CC Combat
-----------------------

*   In [Close Combat](/Close_Combat "Close Combat"), whether in the Active or Reactive Turn, when several members are engaged in the same CC, **only** the Fireteam Leader can be chosen to perform the [CC Roll](/CC_Attack "CC Attack"), applying the [Close Combat with Multiple Troopers](/Close_Combat_with_Multiple_Troopers "Close Combat with Multiple Troopers") rule.
*   In the Reactive Turn, if the Fireteam Leader is not engaged in that CC, the player must choose one of the Fireteam members who are engaged in that CC to perform the CC Roll, applying the Close Combat with Multiple Troopers rule.
*   In [ARO](/ARO "ARO"), the enemy will be able to target any Fireteam member engaged in the CC, but only one of them.

  

Fireteams and Suppressive Fire
------------------------------

*   A Trooper stops being part of a Fireteam if he declares the [Suppressive Fire](/Suppressive_Fire "Suppressive Fire") Common Skill. In addition, a Trooper’s [Suppressive Fire State](/Suppressive_Fire_State "Suppressive Fire State") is automatically cancelled if they join a Fireteam.

  

Fireteams and Comms Attack
--------------------------

*   In the [Reactive Turn](/Reactive_Turn "Reactive Turn"), if the Fireteam has more than one [Hacker](/Hacker "Hacker") and the player declares a Hacking [ARO](/ARO "ARO"), every reactive Hacker may use a different [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program").

  

FAQs
----

N5 FAQ

Version: 0.0.0, Oct 2025

**Q: During your main part of the Deployment Phase, can you reposition Troopers, Deployables, etc?**

A: In their main Deployment Phase step, players can reposition Troopers, Deployables, etc. As soon as a Roll or measurement is made for those game elements, their position is fixed, and they cannot be repositioned during the Deployment Phase.

For example, you can reposition a Trooper as many times as you want, but after you create a Fireteam and adjust the placement of the Fireteam members during the Coherency Check, you can no longer reposition any member of that Fireteam.
