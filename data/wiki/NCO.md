This Special Skill designates the trusted non-commissioned officer of the Lieutenant, who can operate under their direct orders.

NCO

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   Allows the user to replace any [Special Lieutenant Orders](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order") with [Tactical Orders](/Tactical_Order "Tactical Order") and to use them.
*   This Special Skill does not require the NCO and the Lieutenant to be in the same [Combat Group](/Combat_Group "Combat Group").
*   If the Trooper with this Special Skill is a member of a [Fireteam](/Fireteam "Fireteam") and is designated as the Team Leader, then he may use that Tactical Order to activate their Fireteam.

  

![sibylla-n5.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/sibylla-n5.png)

Sibylla's Advice

To use the [Lieutenant Order](/Lieutenant_Special_Order "Lieutenant Special Order"), the Order must have been generated during the [Order Count](/Order_Count "Order Count"). Once generated, the Order may be spent during the [Orders Phase](/Orders_Phase "Orders Phase"), even if the Lieutenant has entered a [Null](/Null "Null") State since then.
