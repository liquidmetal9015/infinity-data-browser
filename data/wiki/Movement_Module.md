* * *

[Moving and Measuring](/Moving_and_Measuring "Moving and Measuring") | [Move](/Move "Move") | [General Movement Rules](/General_Movement_Rules "General Movement Rules") | [Cautious Movement](/Cautious_Movement "Cautious Movement") | [Climb](/Climb "Climb") | [Jump](/Jump "Jump")

  
In Infinity, movement allows troopers to get around the game board. This section details all necessary rules to perform such movements.

Movement takes place within the normal [Order Expenditure Sequence](/Order_Expenditure_Sequence "Order Expenditure Sequence").

The [Movement Attribute (MOV)](/Unit_Profile#Movement_.28MOV.29 "Unit Profile") from the [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile") indicates the number of inches this Trooper can move with an [Order](/Order "Order"). The MOV Attribute usually has two values: the first time the Trooper moves in an Order, and the second.
