WHITE NOISE

SHORT SKILL

[NFB](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   To use this this Hacking Program, the [Hacker](/Hacker "Hacker") must be the Active Trooper.

EFFECTS

*   The White Noise Hacking Program generates a [White Noise Zone](/White_Noise_Zone "White Noise Zone") the size of a Circular Template and with infinite height.
*   On a successful Normal [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll, the [Hacker](/Hacker "Hacker") may place the Circular Template representing the **White Noise Zone** inside their [Hacking Area](/Hacking_Area "Hacking Area") at the end of the Order.
*   The Circular Template must be [totally inside](/Totally_inside "Totally inside") the Hacking Area.
*   The effects of this Program persist until the start of the [States Phase](/States_Phase "States Phase"), at which point you must remove the Circular Template.
*   While the Circular Template is on the game table, the Program's [NFB](/NFB "NFB") Label applies to the Hacker, so they cannot use or apply any other Skill, piece of Equipment, or Hacking Program that has the NFB Label, for example [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism"), [Albedo](/Albedo "Albedo"), [Holoprojector](/Holoprojector "Holoprojector") etc.
*   The range of this Program is the Hacker's Hacking Area.

  

IMPORTANT

Troopers possessing a [Multispectral Visor](/Multispectral_Visor "Multispectral Visor") or [Marksmanship](/Marksmanship "Marksmanship") cannot draw [LoF](/LoF "LoF") through a [White Noise Zone](/White_Noise_Zone "White Noise Zone").
