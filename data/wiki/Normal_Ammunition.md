Normal Ammunition is the conventional form of ammunition and has no special effects.

NORMAL (N) AMMUNITION

ROLL

*   Normal (N) Ammunition forces its target to make one [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") per impact suffered.

EFFECTS

*   Each Saving Roll failed against Normal (N) Ammunition inflicts 1 [Wound](/Wound "Wound") on the target.
*   [Critical](/Critical "Critical") hits with Normal (N) Ammunition cause the target to make an additional Saving Roll.
