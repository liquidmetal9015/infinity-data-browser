This Special Skill indicates the capacity to cancel States when an allied GizmoKit is applied to its user.

TECH-RECOVERY

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Optional](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user must be in a State that can be canceled by the [Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer") Special Skill.
*   The user has received a successful hit from an allied [GizmoKit](/GizmoKit "GizmoKit"), or it has been applied to them through [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact").

EFFECTS

*   By passing a Normal [PH](/PH "PH") Roll, the user cancels all their States that can be canceled by the Engineer Special Skill ([Immobilized-A](/Immobilized-A "Immobilized-A"), [Immobilized-B](/Immobilized-B "Immobilized-B"), [Isolated](/Isolated "Isolated"), [Targeted](/Targeted "Targeted")...), removing the corresponding State Tokens.
*   **Restriction**. This Special Skill does **not** apply to [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious") State, which must be canceled by applying the usual rules.
*   Failing the Normal PH Roll has no negative consequences, and the Roll can be performed again in future Orders.
*   If the user lists GizmoKit in their [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile") with a PH value between brackets, they will apply this PH value when a GizmoKit is used on them.
*   This Special Skill remains active even when its owner is in a [Null](/Null "Null") State.

*   **[Isolated State](/Isolated_State "Isolated State")**

Example

A Trooper with the [Aerial](/Aerial "Aerial") and Tech-Recovery Special Skills is in [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Immobilized-A](/Immobilized-A "Immobilized-A"), and [Isolated](/Isolated "Isolated") States. An ally makes a successful [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") with its [GizmoKit](/GizmoKit "GizmoKit") against them, so they can make a Normal PH Roll to cancel its Unconscious State. Upon passing the Roll, the [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious") State is cancelled and the State Token is removed.

Now, the ally with a GizmoKit spends a new Order and makes another successful BS Attack against the same target, with the intention of canceling the two remaining States, as that Trooper has the Tech-Recovery Special Skill. The Trooper makes a Normal PH Roll and, after passing it, cancels the Immobilized-A and Isolated States, removing the corresponding State Tokens.
