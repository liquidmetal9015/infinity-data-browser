This Ammunition is designed to kill rather than simply incapacitate enemy combatants.

SHOCK AMMUNITION

ROLL

*   After a successful attack using Shock Ammunition, the target must make a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") per hit suffered.

EFFECTS

*   Each Saving Roll failed against Shock Ammunition inflicts 1 [Wound](/Wound "Wound") to its target.
*   [Critical](/Critical "Critical") hits with Shock Ammunition cause the target to make an additional Saving Roll.
*   If the target has the **[VITA](/VITA "VITA") Attribute, with a value of 1**, any failed Saving Rolls against Shock Ammunition will also cancel any type of [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State") the target is in, or has entered during this Order, and the target passes directly to [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State").

  

REMEMBER

Shock Ammunition affects Unconscious State and any Special Skills that affect a Trooper's Unconscious State:

*   [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious")
*   [Shasvastii](/Shasvastii "Shasvastii") and Shasvastii-Embryo
*   [Dogged](/Dogged "Dogged")
*   [No Wound Incapacitation](/No_Wound_Incapacitation "No Wound Incapacitation")

All their special effects are cancelled by Shock Ammunition and those Troopers pass directly to the [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State").
