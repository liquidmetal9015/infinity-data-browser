RETREAT! STATE

ACTIVATION

*   At the beginning of the [Player Turn](/Player_Turn "Player Turn"), the Trooper's army enters the [Retreat! situation](/Retreat!_situation "Retreat! situation").

EFFECTS

*   When the Retreat! situation is declared at the beginning of a player's [Active Turn](/Active_Turn "Active Turn"), that player places a Retreat! Token (RETREAT!) beside each of their surviving troops.
*   The Trooper can only use [Basic Short Skills](/Basic_Short_Skill "Basic Short Skill"), [Cautious Movement](/Cautious_Movement "Cautious Movement"), [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge"), or [Reset](/Reset "Reset") (or any Special Skills that explicitly say so).
*   Troopers in [Holoprojector](/Holoecho_State "Holoecho State") or [HoloMask State](/HoloMask_State "HoloMask State"), or any Marker State, cancel the state when they enter Retreat! State.
*   This state does not interfere with Automatic Special Skills or Automatic Equipment, which will remain functioning normally.
*   Troopers with the [Courage](/Courage "Courage"), [Religious Troop](/Religious_Troop "Religious Troop"), or [Warhorse](/Warhorse "Warhorse") Special Skills, or any other Special Skill that protects them from the effects of the Retreat! situation, do not enter Retreat! State.

CANCELLATION

*   At the beginning of the player's Turn, their army leaves the [Retreat! situation](/Retreat!_situation "Retreat! situation").
*   The Retreat! State of a Trooper can be cancelled by spending a [Command Token](/Command_Token "Command Token"). That Trooper will ignore the effects of Retreat! State until the end of the game.
