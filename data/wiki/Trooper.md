### Attributes

[Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute") are a series of numeric values that describe a Trooper's or game element's basic capabilities. In game terms, these are used to make [Rolls](/Roll "Roll") and calculate the success or failure of attempted actions.

### [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Equipment

Game element with [Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute"), which belongs to the [Army List](/Army_List "Army List") of any of the players and is capable of performing a Special Skill or Trait. This element may also be designated as a target and receive [Attacks](/Attack "Attack").

### [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Weapon

Game element with [Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute") that may belong to the [Army List](/Army_List "Army List") of a player and is capable of performing and receiving [Attacks](/Attack "Attack").

### Marker

Game element with [Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute") represented on the table with a Marker as specified by a Special Skill, Weapon or Equipment.

### Model

Game element with [Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute") represented on the table by a miniature.

### [Peripheral](/Peripheral "Peripheral")

Special category of [Trooper](/Trooper "Trooper") that is unable to contribute or spend [Orders](/Orders "Orders") by itself and, therefore, takes no space in the [Army List](/Army_List "Army List"). It is activated when its Controller, the Trooper on which it depends, spends an Order, replicating their actions. The Cost of a Peripheral is shown in its own Troop Profile but is also added to its Controller's Cost.

### Scenery Element

Game element that recreates the scenarios and serves as a prop on the game table. On certain occasions, they may have [Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute") or become a valid target.

### State Token

Game element that indicates the effect of a rule, [Skill](/Skill "Skill"), or [State](/State "State"), as a reminder for the player.

### Target

Game element capable of being targeted by [Attacks](/Attack "Attack") and effects from Skills or Equipment.

### Token

Game element that represents a piece of [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Equipment or a [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Weapon.

### Trooper

Game element with [Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute") which belongs to the [Army List](/Army_List "Army List") of any player, able to contribute and spend [Orders](/Orders "Orders"), as well as declaring and receiving [Attacks](/Attack "Attack").

### [Unit Profile](/Unit_Profile "Unit Profile")

Represents the set of Troopers that form each Unit in Infinity. Indicates the numerical values that determine each Trooper's [Attributes](/Attribute "Attribute"). The Unit Profile also indicates the Characteristics, Special Skills, Equipment, and Armor of each Trooper.

### Victory Points

The combined [Cost](/Cost "Cost") of the player's Troopers who are in a non-[Null](/Null "Null") state.
