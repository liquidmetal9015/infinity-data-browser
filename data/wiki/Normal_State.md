NORMAL STATE

ACTIVATION

*   Unless otherwise noted, all Troopers and game elements ("Trooper" from now on) deploy in this state.
*   Troopers in any [Null](/Null "Null") State revert to the Normal State if their Null States are cancelled.
*   Therefore, Troopers in [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State") revert to Normal State if a [Wound](/Wound "Wound") is removed.

EFFECTS

*   The Trooper generates 1 [Order](/Order "Order") for their player to use during the [Tactical Phase](/Tactical_Phase "Tactical Phase").
*   The Trooper counts towards the player's [Victory Points](/Victory_Points "Victory Points").

CANCELLATION

*   Normal State is canceled when the Trooper enters any [Null](/Null "Null") State ([Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious"), [Dead](/Dead "Dead")...) or when the effect of a Special Skill, weapon or piece of Equipment indicates so.
