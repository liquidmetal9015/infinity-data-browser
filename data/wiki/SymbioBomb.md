The SymbioBomb is a single-use weapon that allows its owner, a Trooper whose Unit Profile must list the SymbioBomb, to assign it to another Trooper in the same army, who is known as the user, in order to grant the latter additional attack and support advantages.

SYMBIOBOMB

[Assignable (Transmutation), Comms Attack, Optional.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   By expending one [Short Skill](/Short_Skill "Short Skill")/[ARO](/ARO "ARO"), the user can use one Pheroware Tactic: Endgame, Eraser, or Mirrorball (see [Weapon Chart](/Weapon_Chart#PHEROWARE_TACTICS_.28PT.29 "Weapon Chart")).
*   When using the Endgame or Eraser Pheroware Tactics with a SymbioBomb, a -3 MOD is applied to the target of the [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack") in any [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll").
*   The SymbioBomb is removed from the game table at the end of the Order in which it was used.

  

Example of SymbioBomb

During the [Deployment Phase](/Deployment_Phase "Deployment Phase"), the Tohaa Player assigns its Kaeltar Specialist’s SymbioBombs to a Sakiel and to a Gorgos, who already had a SymbioMate.
