TRIANGULATED FIRE

LONG SKILL

[Attack, Optional.](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user must have [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to the target.

EFFECTS

*   Allows the user to declare a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") against a target without applying **any MODs** to the Roll ([Range](/Range "Range"), [Cover](/Cover "Cover"), Special Skills such as [Mimetism](/Mimetism "Mimetism")...).
*   The only exception are MODs that affect [Burst](/Burst "Burst") (B), which are applied normally.

  

IMPORTANT

Triangulated Fire does not allow the user to hit a target farther away than the weapon’s Maximum [Range](/Range "Range"). For example, a Combi Rifle can never hit a target at a distance of more than 48 inches.

N5 FAQ

Version: 0.0.0, Oct 2025

**Q: Do Skills that let you perform a 'BS Attack Roll' (for example Intuitive Attack and Speculative Attack), or have the BS Attack Label, count as a BS Attack for MODs? For example, if the Trooper has 'BS Attack (SR-1)', 'BS Attack (+1 SD), or 'BS Attack (AP)'.**

A: Yes. Note that +1 SD MODs don't apply to Long Skills.
