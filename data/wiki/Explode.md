This Special Skill marks the Trooper's capacity to detonate in a blast

EXPLODE

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Attack, Obligatory.](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

*   The user must be in [Unconscious State](/Unconscious_State "Unconscious State") for this Special Skill to activate.

EFFECTS

*   This Special Skill forces its user to detonate automatically when he enters Unconscious state.
*   At the end of the Order in which the user entered Unconscious State, before making [Guts Rolls](/Guts_Rolls "Guts Rolls"), the Trooper makes a [Direct Template](/Direct_Template "Direct Template") Attack, placing the center of the Circular Template (Blast Focus) over the center of their base.
*   Explode applies [Shock Ammunition](/Shock_Ammunition "Shock Ammunition"), forcing targets to make a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") against ARM, with PS7.
*   Enemy Troopers who are affected by the Attack and had performed a [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge") will avoid this Attack if they passed their PH Roll.
*   Once all Rolls have been made, the user enters [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State") and is removed from the game table.

  

IMPORTANT

If a Trooper enters [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State") directly due to a Special Skill (e.g. [Dogged](/Dogged "Dogged")), an Ammunition (e.g. [Shock](/Shock "Shock")), or because he suffers several [Wounds](/Wounds "Wounds") in a single Order, this Special Skill does not activate.

If the user activates the Dogged or [No Wound Incapacitation](/No_Wound_Incapacitation "No Wound Incapacitation") Special Skills when entering Unconscious State, he does not Explode.

  

REMEMBER

Template Attacks that affect [allied](/Allied "Allied") Models or Markers are canceled, but the user of Explode still enters [Dead State](/Dead_State "Dead State").
