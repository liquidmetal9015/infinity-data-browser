Basic Common Skill that allows Troopers to move about the battlefield.

MOVE

BASIC SHORT SKILL

[Movement, No LoF, No Roll.](/Labels "Labels")

REQUIREMENTS

Players must check the Requirements of this Skill when declaring it.

*   At least half the Trooper's base must stay in contact with the surface on which they are moving, except when vaulting over obstacles (see [General Movement Rules](/General_Movement_Rules "General Movement Rules")).
*   Any surface they move on must be at least half as wide as their base.
*   The Trooper must be able to finish their movement on a surface that's equal to or larger than their base.

EFFECTS

*   Allows the user to traverse the battlefield up to a distance determined by his [MOV](/MOV "MOV") [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute").
*   By declaring Move, the user may move up to the first value of his MOV Attribute in inches.
*   If the user declares Move again as their second Basic Short Skill of the Order, they may then move up to the second value of their MOV Attribute in inches.
*   Move must follow the [General Movement Rules](/General_Movement_Rules "General Movement Rules") as well as the [Moving and Measuring](/Moving_and_Measuring "Moving and Measuring") rules.
