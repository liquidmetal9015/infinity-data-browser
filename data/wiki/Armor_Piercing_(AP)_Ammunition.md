A type of ammunition designed to penetrate even the toughest armor plating.

ARMOR-PIERCING (AP) AMMUNITION

ROLL

*   After a successful attack using AP Ammunition, the target must make a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") per hit suffered, using half his [ARM](/ARM "ARM") or [BTS](/BTS "BTS") value (ARM/2 or BTS/2) as specified by the weapon being used.

EFFECTS

*   AP Ammunition reduces the ARM or BTS value of its target to half of its original value, rounding up. Consequently, any ARM or BTS above 0 cannot be reduced below 1.
*   Each [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") failed against AP Ammunition inflicts 1 [Wound](/Wound "Wound") on the target.
*   [Critical](/Critical "Critical") hits with AP Ammunition cause the targets to make an additional Saving Roll, applying their halved ARM or BTS value.
