CHEST MINES

EFFECTS

*   This weapon has two Modes of use:
    *   In **BS Weapon Mode**, Chest Mines function as a [Direct Template Weapon](/Direct_Template_Weapon "Direct Template Weapon"), not applying the Mines rule.
    *   In **CC Weapon Mode**, Chest Mines function as a [CC Weapon](/CC_Weapon "CC Weapon"), not applying the Mines rule. If the user wins the [Face to Face Roll](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll"), the Chest Mine will affect all enemy Troopers in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with its bearer. This weapon cannot be used in CC if there are [allied](/Allied "Allied") troopers in Silhouette contact with its bearer.
*   The [Disposable](/Disposable "Disposable") (2) Trait is shared between all Modes. The bearer of this weapon has only two uses available, no matter which Modes are used.
*   The detonation of a Chest Mine doesn't affect its bearer.
