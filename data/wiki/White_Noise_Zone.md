VISIBILITY CONDITIONS

Due to thick vegetation, jagged rocks, snow, sandstorms and any number of other reasons, some areas obscure a soldier’s vision and his ability to aim accurately. This is represented by the following game rules. Vision-obscuring areas are divided into four categories:

*   Low Visibility Zone
*   Poor Visibility Zone
*   Zero Visibility Zone
*   White Noise Zone

EFFECTS

*   Any Skill, Special Skill or piece of Equipment that requires [LoF](/LoF "LoF"), except [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge"), and is performed from, into, or through a Visibility Zone suffers a MOD to the relevant Attribute in the required Roll.
*   This MOD for Visibility stacks with other MODs for Special Skills, Equipment, [Partial Cover](/Partial_Cover "Partial Cover"), [Range](/Range "Range")... but never with other Zone of Visibility MODs.

### Low Visibility Zone

*   Low Visibility Zones apply a MOD of -3.

  

### Poor Visibility Zone

*   Poor Visibility Zones apply a MOD of -6.

  

### Zero Visibility Zone

*   [LoF](/LoF "LoF") cannot be drawn into, through, or out of a Zero Visibility Zone.
*   Any Trooper who is the target of a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") into, through, or out of a Zero Visibility Zone treats the Zero Visibility Zone as a Poor Visibility Zone when drawing LoF to the attacker. _[(See FAQs.)](#FAQs)_

SEE ALSO

**[Smoke Ammunition](/Smoke_Ammunition "Smoke Ammunition")**

*   Smoke Ammunition generates a [Zero Visibility Zone](/Zero_Visibility_Zone "Zero Visibility Zone") (see: Special Terrain, Visibility Conditions) the size of a Circular Template and with infinite height.

  
**[Eclipse Ammunition](/Eclipse_Ammunition "Eclipse Ammunition")**

*   Eclipse Ammunition works in exactly the same way as [Smoke Ammunition](/Smoke_Ammunition "Smoke Ammunition"), but the [Zero Visibility Zone](/Zero_Visibility_Zone "Zero Visibility Zone") it generates also affects [Multispectral Visors](/Multispectral_Visor "Multispectral Visor"), preventing them from drawing [LoF](/LoF "LoF") through the Zone, regardless of their Level.

  

### White Noise Zone

*   A White Noise Zone acts as a Zero Visibility Zone, but only for Troopers with the [Marksmanship](/Marksmanship "Marksmanship") Special Skill or equipped with a [Multispectral Visor](/Multispectral_Visor "Multispectral Visor") of any Level, or any other piece of Equipment that specifies the same.
*   If a Trooper with a Multispectral Visor is the target of a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack") into, through, or out of a White Noise Zone, their Multispectral Visor cannot reduce the MODs of the resulting Poor Visibility Zone.

SEE ALSO

**[White Noise](/White_Noise "White Noise") Hacking Program**

*   The White Noise Hacking Program generates a [White Noise Zone](/White_Noise_Zone "White Noise Zone") the size of a Circular Template and with infinite height.

  

IMPORTANT

Visibility Zone MODs never stack with other Visibility Zone MODs. If any Roll would be affected by two or more Visibility Zones of whatever type, players will apply only one MOD, that must be always the most restrictive one.

For example, if a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack")’s [LoF](/LoF "LoF") passes through a Low Visibility Zone (-3 MOD) and a Poor Visibility Zone (-6 MOD), apply only one single -6 MOD.

  

Visibility Zones and Speculative Attack
---------------------------------------

[Speculative Attacks](/Speculative_Attack "Speculative Attack") do not require [LoF](/LoF "LoF"), so do not apply any negative MODs for Visibility Zones, only the -6 MOD for Speculative Attack.

Visibility Conditions and Combat Jump
-------------------------------------

You cannot use [Combat Jump](/Combat_Jump "Combat Jump") inside of, or in contact with, an area with Low, Poor, or Zero Visibility Conditions.

N5 FAQ

Version: 0.0.0, Oct 2025

**Q: How do Sixth Sense, Zero Visibility Zones and Multipectral Visor Level 1 interact?**

A: If the Trooper with Multispectral Visor Level 1 and Sixth Sense is the target of a BS Attack through a Zero Visibility Zone, they ignore the -6 MOD for drawing LoF through the Zero Visibility Zone.
