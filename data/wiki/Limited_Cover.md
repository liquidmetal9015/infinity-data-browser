LIMITED COVER

AUTOMATIC SKILL

[Obligatory.](/Labels "Labels")

EFFECTS

*   Attacks against this Trooper do **not** apply the **-3 [BS](/BS "BS") MOD for [Partial Cover](/Partial_Cover "Partial Cover")**.

  

![sibylla-n5.png](https://assets.corvusbelli.net/wiki/n5/sibylla-n5.png)

Sibylla's Advice

Keep in mind that the most severe restriction always applies. If a **[No Cover](/No_Cover "No Cover")** Trooper also has **Limited Cover** due to another rule or Skill, the No Cover Skill will still apply.
