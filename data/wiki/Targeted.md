TARGETED STATE

ACTIVATION

*   The Trooper or game element ("Trooper" from now on) suffers a successful [Attack](/Attack "Attack") or Effect using an [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Ammunition"), [Hacking Program](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program") or a game condition or Scenario Special Rule, capable of causing this state.

EFFECTS

*   Any Trooper declaring a [BS Attack](/BS_Attack "BS Attack"), [Comms Attack](/Comms_Attack "Comms Attack"), or [Discover](/Discover "Discover") against a Trooper in Targeted State gains a **+3 MOD** to the [Attribute](/Attribute "Attribute") used to perform that Skill.
*   Troopers in this state must apply a -3 WIP MOD to their **[Reset](/Reset "Reset")** rolls.
*   **Restriction:** While in this state, **[Cautious Movement](/Cautious_Movement "Cautious Movement")** may not be declared.
*   **Restriction:** A Trooper in this state may not use the **[Stealth](/Stealth "Stealth")** Special Skill.
*   This state does not interfere with Automatic Special Skills or Automatic Equipment, which will function normally.

CANCELLATION

*   The affected Trooper successfully passes a [Normal](/Normal "Normal") or [Face to Face](/Face_to_Face_Roll "Face to Face Roll") [Reset](/Reset "Reset") Roll, applying a -3 [WIP](/WIP "WIP") MOD.
*   A Trooper with the **[Engineer](/Engineer "Engineer")** Special Skill (or an equivalent Skill) may cancel the state by spending one [Short Skill](/Short_Skill "Short Skill") of an [Order](/Trooper_Activation "Trooper Activation") while in [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with the affected Trooper, and passing a [Normal](/Normal "Normal") [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll (or the Roll specified by the Special Skill or Scenario).

  

SEE ALSO

**[Spotlight](/Spotlight "Spotlight")**

*   Any failed [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") causes the target to enter Targeted State, placing a Targeted State Token (TARGETED) by them.

  
**[Forward Observer](/Forward_Observer "Forward Observer")**

*   The use of Forward Observer is a form of Attack with a [BS Weapon](/BS_Weapon "BS Weapon") with the [BS Weapon (WIP)](/BS_Weapon_\(WIP\) "BS Weapon (WIP)") Trait, and instead of forcing the target to make a [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll"), causes them to enter Targeted State.
