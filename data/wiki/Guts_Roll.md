When Troopers survive a successful Attack, it means their body armor took the brunt of the impact, keeping them from serious injury. This triggers a primal fear of death, and survival instincts kick in. The Troopers' survival instincts can force them to recoil from danger and dive for cover.

GUTS ROLL

REQUIREMENTS

*   The Trooper is not in an [Engaged](/Engaged "Engaged"), [Null](/Null "Null") or [IMM](/IMM "IMM") State.
*   The Trooper had suffered one or more successful Attacks that cause the Trooper to enter any [States](/State "State") or to perform any [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Rolls "Saving Rolls").
*   The Guts Roll is performed even if all the attackers are in a [Null](/Null "Null") State at the end of the Order.

EFFECTS

*   A Guts Roll consists in a single [Normal](/Normal_Roll "Normal Roll") [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll, always performed at the very end of the Order, after performing any [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll").
*   If the Guts Roll is successful, the Trooper must stand its ground and must do nothing else.
*   If a Trooper would have to make a Guts Roll, its owner can instead choose to fail it automatically.
*   **If the WIP Roll is failed**, it is compulsory to perform the first of these options that the Trooper can complete:
    *   The Trooper must move up to 2 inches to **completely leave**:
        *   The [LoF](/LoF "LoF") of the Enemy or Enemies (for example, in the case of a [Coordinated Order](/Coordinated_Order "Coordinated Order"), or multiple [AROs](/ARO "ARO")) that have performed Attacks against the Trooper.
        *   The danger zone or Area of Effect of Attacks that did not require LoF.
    *   If this is not possible, the Trooper must move up to 2 inches to gain [Total or Partial Cover](/Cover "Cover") from all attackers.
    *   If the two previous options are not possible, the Trooper must go [Prone](/Prone "Prone"), facing in any direction its player chooses
*   Check the enemy LoF or Area of Effect from the Trooper's final position at the end of the Order, when the Guts Roll is made.
*   When checking the enemy's LoF or Area of Effect, if the Trooper is already outside them and the Guts Roll is failed, the Trooper enters Prone State, facing in a direction of the player's choice.
*   This movement of a failed Guts Roll:
    *   Does **not** generate [AROs](/ARO "ARO") or activate [Deployable](/Deployable "Deployable") Weapons or Equipment.
    *   Must follow the [General Movement Rules](/General_Movement_Rules "General Movement Rules") as well as the [Moving and Measuring](/Moving_and_Measuring "Moving and Measuring") rules.
    *   Does **not** allow the Trooper to enter [Silhouette contact](/Silhouette_contact "Silhouette contact") with enemy Troopers, or with Scenario Objectives.

  

IMPORTANT

Attacks that have no PS value and do not cause any State do not require a Guts Roll.

  

REMEMBER

Troopers in [Engaged State](/Engaged_State "Engaged State") do not make Guts Rolls.

  

SEE ALSO

**[Stun Ammunition](/Stun_Ammunition "Stun Ammunition")**

*   Additionally, failing the [Saving Roll](/Saving_Roll "Saving Roll") causes the target to automatically fail the subsequent Guts Roll for having survived an Attack, unless he has the [Courage](/Courage "Courage") Special Skill or an equivalent.

  
**[Climbing Plus](/Climbing_Plus "Climbing Plus")**

*   Climbing Plus also allows the user to move on vertical surfaces as if performing normal Movement on a horizontal surface when declaring [Move](/Move "Move") or [Dodge](/Dodge "Dodge"), or during movement due to a failed Guts Roll. The [General Movement Rules](/General_Movement_Rules "General Movement Rules") and [Moving and Measuring](/Moving_and_Measuring "Moving and Measuring") chapter must be applied.

  
**[Courage](/Courage "Courage")**

*   Courage allows its user to choose to automatically pass any Guts Rolls.

  
**[Religious Troop](/Religious_Troop "Religious Troop")**

*   The user automatically passes all their Guts Rolls without requiring a Roll. This means that the user is forced to hold their position and cannot withdraw or seek cover after surviving an Attack.
*   However, if the player wants the user to withdraw or gain [Cover](/Cover "Cover") after surviving an Attack, then they may make a [WIP](/WIP "WIP") Roll. If the Roll is a success, the user can apply the Effects of failing the Guts Roll.
