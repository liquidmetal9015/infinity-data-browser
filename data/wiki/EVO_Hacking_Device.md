Hacking Devices are the tools used during cybercombat. Multipurpose and versatile, these devices can be useful as support elements or to perform powerful cyberattacks, depending on the Device.

  

SEE ALSO

From [Quantronic Combat (Hacking)](/Quantronic_Combat_\(Hacking\)#Hacking_Devices_and_Hacking_Programs:_Characteristics "Quantronic Combat (Hacking)").

Hacking Devices and Hacking Programs have the following characteristics:

*   They act in the user's [Hacking Area](/Hacking_Area "Hacking Area").
*   They do not require [LoF](/LoF "LoF") to act, unless the Hacking Program's own description states otherwise.
*   Only Troopers in Model form may be targeted by [Hacking Programs](/Hacking_Program "Hacking Program").
*   They benefit, if applicable, from the target's [Targeted](/Targeted "Targeted") State.
