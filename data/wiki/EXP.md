A type of ordnance designed to cause massive damage to the target by detonating on impact.

EXPLOSIVE (EXP) AMMUNITION

ROLL

*   After a successful attack using Explosive (EXP) Ammunition, the target must make three [Saving Rolls](/Saving_Rolls "Saving Rolls") per hit suffered.

EFFECTS

*   Each Saving Roll failed against EXP Ammunition inflicts 1 [Wound](/Wound "Wound") on the target.
*   The three Saving Rolls are mandatory, even if the target fails one of them or falls [Unconscious](/Unconscious "Unconscious").
*   [Critical](/Critical "Critical") hits with Explosive Ammunition cause the target to make an additional Saving Roll.

Sibylla's Advice

For each Critical hit with EXP Ammunition, the target must make 4 Saving Rolls: 3 due to the impact with EXP Ammunition and 1 more due to the additional Saving Roll.
